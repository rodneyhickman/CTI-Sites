Our Faculty

Every CTI leader has completed our Core Curriculum, Certification Programme and intensive year-long Co-active Leadership Programme. After a subsequent rigorous leader audition, selected leaders must also complete a six-month series of advanced coaching and leadership practicum while maintaining an active coaching practice. We have the most rigorous leader training requirements of any coach training school - and it shows!

We invite you to explore the commitment, lives and passions of our CTI leaders. Each brings a unique perspective and excitement to their teaching and all are guides on your unique journey of self-exploration toward a successful coaching career!

"I feel very grateful that I had the privilege of being trained by CTI. That has empowered me, and has made it possible for me to build a coaching practice."

Wim Vermaak, Executive Co-active Coach
Amsterdam, The Netherlands
