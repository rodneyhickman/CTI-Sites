The Coaches Console

The Coaches Console is a popular online practice management system that quickly reduces your administrative burden so you can grow your coaching practice! This easy-to-use system conquers the administrative overload of coaching, letting you focus on clients - not paperwork!

Assessment Tools

These tools contain areas that, viewed together, represent one way of describing a whole life or professional career. These tools measure a level of satisfaction and range of expression in certain areas as a snapshot in time. As your clients work through their Assessment Wheels they will discover areas in which they can acknowledge their success and areas in which they may want to improve their level of satisfaction.

Download the Personal and Professional Wheels to use with your clients!
