The brain has “plasticity” – can grow and change throughout our lifetime – and few people realize that Co-Active coaching is a catalyst. In this experiential workshop, you’ll learn about the latest brain research into how we think, feel and change, and explore what actually works to create real, lasting transformation.

Whether you’re a coach, a coaching client or just fascinated by these ideas, you will take home some powerful insights and effective brain-based tools you can use immediately.

About Ann Betz

Ann Betz is an expert in the neuroscience of leadership, coaching, and human consciousness. In her capacity as CTI’s neuroscience consultant, she is actively engaged in making connections between the Co-Active model and current research on the brain. Ann is the co-founder of BEabove Leadership, which focuses on helping individuals, non-profits and businesses remember their innate capacity to lead, work and live “above the line,” thus increasing their effectiveness and engagement. Ann works internationally in the corporate and non-profit/NGO sectors, bringing her unique brand of humor, playfulness and deep learning to organizations and communities around the world.
