Presence: Authentic Public Speaking for Co-Active Coaches

 

Learn the basics of confident authentic public speaking with authenticity and presence.

 

With Daniel Kingsley and Millie Baker of Presence Training
In London on the 30th of October 2013

The skills Co-Active coaches learn are usually applied within the context of the coach/client relationship. But they are also relevant for speaking to groups, be this in formal talks, presentations, meetings or facilitation.

This one day taster workshop builds on your co-active skills, looking at how holding our attention at level one can build confidence, and introducing a new perspective on the being and the doing of public speaking.

Millie and Daniel create a safe and open space for you to explore, experiment and have fun speaking, and develop more of the skills you need to fulfil your life purpose.

Time:  9:45am to 5:00pm

In this workshop we introduce, practice and embed the fundamental building blocks of authentic public speaking.

We focus on the simple experience of standing in front of and relating to the group. It isn’t about having something impressive or entertaining to say (in fact, you don’t have to speak at all if you don’t want to), it’s about relaxing and being yourself. That means there is nothing to prepare in advance.

  • Allow your saboteurs to go to tea
  • Gain new insights into the relationship between being and doing in public speaking
  • Understand the connection between body geography and confidence
  • Connect with your listeners from a more grounded place
  • Express yourself with authenticity and clarity
  • Give and receive appreciation

Come and be surprised, delighted and inspired by a new way of speaking.

Presence Training
Millie and Daniel are the co-founders of Presence Training Ltd.   Their mission is to help people speak in public, give presentations and communicate with natural presence and deep self-confidence.

Daniel Kingsley

Daniel worked as a barrister for 15 years and became very accustomed to speaking in public and speaking persuasively.  He spoke regularly not only in court but to audiences large and small, including the UN Human Rights Council.   He has been exploring the world of authentic speaking since 2007, and has now given up his practice in law in order to coach it full-time.

 

Millie Baker

Millie has been facilitating groups in authentic communication since 1999. She has trained and coached for a wide variety of clients including SAP, Daimler-Chrysler, Muller Milk, German Cancer Research Centre, and the Universities of Berlin, Frankfurt, Munich, Heidelberg, Vienna and Zurich. Millie is currently training to become a Co-Active coach.

 

“This work helps you tap into you, and the impact that you want to create in public speaking – it allows you to speak from a genuine place. It’s not just standing up and speaking but speaking from that resonant place inside. It’s about that depth – there is no right way, there’s your way. Everyone could benefit from these workshops – if you feel comfortable standing up and talking to a group, you can do anything.”

Sonia Calvo – CTI Certified Coach

“This work has made a huge impact on my ability to speak to audiences in both work and social contexts, to be myself, without fear, and not to rely on a scaffold of preparation even under high pressure. I can’t begin to describe how liberating this has been for me.”

Daniel, Senior Director and Legal Counsel – Technology Company
