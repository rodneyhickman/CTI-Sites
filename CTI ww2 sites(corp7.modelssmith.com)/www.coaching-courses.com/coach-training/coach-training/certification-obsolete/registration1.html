Registration

Step 1: Register Early

  • Download the Early Registration request form.
  • Select a month to begin the programme, submit the Programme Registration Request form and pay the deposit. The pre-register date determines your place in the queue when that programme is being filled. To keep your place in the queue you still must submit a completed application by the deadline.

Step 2: Application

  • Download the Information Packet and Official Application.
  • Submit the application along with your certified coach. It's due the 1st of the month before the month you want to start. For example, if you want to start in February, then your application is due January 1st.
  • Get your application in early if you have not already pre-registered for a specific month. Spaces are limited each month and applications are taken on a first come first serve basis.
  • Submit your completed application along your required deposit to:
    CTI
    Attn: Certification Programme
    4000 Civic Center Drive Suite 500
    San Rafael, CA 94903
    Or fax your completed application to: 415-472-1204

Step 3: Acceptance

Once your application has been received and reviewed, you will be notified by CTI regarding acceptance into the Certification Programme and your next steps.

Programme Fees

If you have not previously registered, the Certification enrollment fee is $4,620.00, which is comprised of a deposit of $710.00 due at the time of registration, and a balance of $3,910.00. (If you previously registered into a discount package then your pricing is different and your deposit is already on file.)

If a third party is paying for your programme, they must provide a credit card or pay the balance in full up front. The funds can be sent by wire transfer directly to our Bank; however, monthly payments being made by wire transfers are not acceptable. Contact our Certification Programme Specialist for wiring instructions.

The balance is due as follows: the first of five automatic credit card charges will take place the 1st of the month following the start date of your programme and approximately every 30 days thereafter. These payments will be charged to the credit card you have provided at the time of registration. Your account must be current for you to register for your exam. CTI reserves the right to dismiss you from the programme if your account is more than 30 days in arrears.
