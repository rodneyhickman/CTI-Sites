Weekend Course Times

The following course times apply to all weekend courses (Friday, Saturday, and Sunday).
Please keep in mind that our courses build on one another and must be taken in the following order.

  • Co-active Coaching Fundamentals: Day1: 2pm - 6pm, Day 2: 10am - 6pm, Day 3: 9am - 5pm
  • Fulfillment: Day1 & 2: 10am - 6pm, Day 3: 9am - 5pm
  • Balance: Day1 & 2: 10am - 6pm, Day 3: 9am - 5pm
  • Process: Day1 & 2: 10am - 6pm, Day 3: 9am - 5pm
  • Synergy: Day1 & 2: 10am - 6pm, Day 3: 9am - 5pm

Mid-week Course Times

  • Co-active Coaching Fundamentals: Day1: 2pm - 6pm, Day 2 & 3: 10am - 6pm
  • Fulfillment: All days: 10pm - 6pm
  • Balance: All days: 10pm - 6pm
  • Process: All days: 10pm - 6pm
  • Synergy: All days: 10pm - 6pm

Please note: CTI may cancel or reschedule courses at its discretion. CTI will not be responsible for costs that may be incurred by students as a result of such cancellations or rescheduling. Additional courses are added based on increased enrollment.
