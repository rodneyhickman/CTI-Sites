Space Available

Transfers and Cancellations, Refund Policies/Processing Fees, and Cancellations & Transfers:

If you choose to cancel your enrollment in a Leadership program, you will be charged a processing fee according to the schedule below. Processing fees are in US dollars for US programs and in Euros for Spain programs. Fees for transfers and cancellations will be deducted from the program deposit. The transfer policy does not apply when transferring into a program for which you have been wait-listed.

  • More than 60 days prior to your start date: transfer fee $100 cancellation fee $200.
  • Less than 60 days prior to your start date: transfer fee $175 cancellation fee $250.

A discount for five or more participants from one tribe reduces individual tuition to $1,950

Any balance due will be charged 21 days prior to the retreat date.

Conference Center Location Description
Westerbeke Ranch Conference Center is located in Valley of the Moon, the heart of Sonoma Valley Wine Country.
The lush natural beauty of acres of native oaks and private garden paths provide a nurturing and peaceful setting for our retreat.
Gourmet meals are served in a beautiful California mission styled dining room.
