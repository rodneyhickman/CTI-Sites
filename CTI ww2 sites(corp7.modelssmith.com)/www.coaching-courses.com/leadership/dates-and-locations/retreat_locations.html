Retreat Locations

CTI's Co-active® Leadership training program encompasses four residential retreats held over a 10-month period, as well as in-between projects.

Sitges, Spain

Situated just south of Barcelona, this luxuriously restored masia (Catalan manor house) sits high on a hillside in the Pinedes wine region surrounded by the Garaf National Park, offering dramatic sea or mountain views from every room and terrace. Complemented by high tech comforts, this tranquil haven retains its charm and elegant character with more than 300 acres of vineyards, olive groves and fruit trees while being only 10 minutes from the lively beach resort of Sitges. Fly into Barcelona International Airport (BCN).

Northern California, USA

All retreats are held at a beautiful, modern meeting space nestled amid acres of majestic coastal redwoods in Santa Cruz County. The Sequoia land has been held sacred since 1949. The Sequoia Retreat Center is a place where the deepest meaning of life may be explored in an atmosphere of quiet and beauty. People from all over the world come here to reflect on, renew and reenergize the meaning and purpose of their lives and work. This spectacular setting is approximately ten miles inland from the Pacific Ocean. Fly directly into San Jose International Airport (SJC) or San Francisco International Airport (SFO).

North Carolina, USA

This beautifully renovated retreat center lodge-formerly a tobacco ranch-is cradled in the Blue Ridge Mountains of Western North Carolina. The lodge is the state's first eco-conscious, carbon neutral retreat center in the western region, nestled on 63 acres of land that slope gently down to the Ivy River. Tastefully decorated with art from around the world, the retreat center lodge is finished in warm pine and other creative touches. Surrounded by a lush natural environment with forest trails, a pond, waterfall, and gardens, this lodge provides a serene and peaceful escape. Fly directly into Asheville Regional (AVL) or via Charlotte Douglas International (CLT) Airport.

In choosing a programme, please be sure that you can attend all four retreats of that programme. You will have the same leaders and fellow participants throughout the course of the programme.

In order to be put on the waiting list for a full programme, you must be registered in another open programme.
