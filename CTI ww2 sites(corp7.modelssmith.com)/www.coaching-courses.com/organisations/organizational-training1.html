Organisational Training

The Coaches Training Institute’s Internal Co-Active Coach® curriculum, designed specifically for coaches working within an organisation, builds a solid foundation of Co-Active Coaching® skills and can lead to a coaching credential with the International Coach Federation (ICF).

Who Should Attend
The Internal Co-Active Coach curriculum is tailored specifically for human resource, organisational development, and external professionals whose role is to provide formal or informal coaching inside organisations.

Outcomes
Participants will learn to:

  • Apply a wide range of skills for coaching leaders or colleagues to:
  • Clarify high-leverage strategic goals and aspirations
  • Explore options from a range of perspectives and make effective choices
  • Lead with greater presence and self-awareness
  • Act with greater boldness and alignment in pursuit of important goals
  • Bring forward their most authentic coaching style using the Co-Active Coaching Model®
  • Plan and implement a coaching engagement from start to completion
  • Use assessment data to support the coaching process
  • Plan strategies to measure the impact of a coaching engagement
  • Make sound ethical and legal judgments throughout the coaching process
