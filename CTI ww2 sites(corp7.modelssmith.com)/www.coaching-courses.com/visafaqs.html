Visa FAQs

FAQs

Where do I go to get my visa?

The UK Border Agency http://www.ukba.homeoffice.gov.uk/visas-immigration/

What type of visa do I need?

A student visitor visa

If I need assistance, who can help me with the process?

The Home Office UK Border Agency can help you find the right form, provide guidance and check that your application is complete  https://apply.ukba.homeoffice.gov.uk/iapply.portal

How long does the application take to be approved?

Please refer to the guide to visa processing times  http://www.ukba.homeoffice.gov.uk/visas-immigration/general-info/processing-times/

Can CTI provide me with proof of registration?

Yes, please contact CTI Customer Service at UKRegistration@thecoaches.com.    The letter will summarize payment, course registration, course dates and total hours.

If my application is declined will CTI reimburse me for the application fees?

No, CTI will not provide reimbursement
