Podcasts - Required Listening

These four Podcasts feature CTI faculty members discussing different topics that are important and at times challenging for coaches in Certification. The Podcasts are unscripted, as leaders are speaking from their own experience as coaches and as CTI faculty. Each Podcast is one hour long.


• Arcs of coaching learn about the arcs of the client relationship from discovery through completion and the importance of the designed alliance along the way.


• Accountability: Learn what is important about accountability and different approaches to homework, structures and accountability overall.


• Saboteur: This Podcast discusses how to know when the adaptable voices of the Saboteur are present, and how to reconnect back to the resonance of the whole client.


• Evoking Transformation: Explore the “Co” and “Active” of of this critical Cornerstone…what are YOU evoking?

The Dip - Optional Listening

The Dip Podcast

Learning something new like coaching can be exciting and it can be challenging. It is very common for Certification students to have a period of time in the Program where it all just feels like too much. If you are feeling like you are not ever going to “get” coaching, listen to The Dip.
