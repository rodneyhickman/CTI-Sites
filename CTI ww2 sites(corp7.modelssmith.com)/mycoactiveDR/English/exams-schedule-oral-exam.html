Scheduling Your Oral Exam

Plan ahead for your Oral Exam as exam dates are often booked 60+ days in advance. You must complete the Oral Exam within one year of the start date of your Certification Program.

Your oral exam date will be scheduled so that it occurs at least 30 days after you will have completed and documented your 100 coaching hours.

You may schedule your Oral Exam after you have:

  • • Completed your Mid-Program Review with your CPL
  • • Completed at least 70 of your 100 required coaching hours
  • • You can effectively determine when your 100 hours will be completed

At this time, you must schedule your Oral Exam by phone with CTI Customer Service at (415) 451-6000, Option 1 or Toll Free in the U.S. at (800) 691-6008, Option 1. Exams are not scheduled via email.

In order to hold your exam date, you will be required to pay for your Oral Exam in full at the time that you schedule if it was not included in your program price.
