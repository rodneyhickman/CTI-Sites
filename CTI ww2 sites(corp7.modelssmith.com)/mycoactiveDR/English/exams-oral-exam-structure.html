Oral Exam Structure

    The Oral Exam takes place over the telephone and lasts approximately two hours. There is an opening “circle” where you, the other examinees and your examiners will introduce yourselves. Once everyone is introduced, the lead examiner will describe the logistics and you will have a chance to ask questions about the process.

    Following the opening circle, each examinee will call in for their individual oral exam at the time and telephone number that will be designated for them. The individual oral exam is comprised of two coaching sessions; each coaching session will be 15 minutes long and will be recorded. Each of your two examiners will be your client for one coaching session. Your examiners will be real clients, with issues that are authentic for them at the time of your exam.

    You will be assessed on your ability to demonstrate competent Co-Active Coaching. Most importantly, we expect you to create a sense of Co-Active engagement during your coaching session.

    Approximately 90 minutes after the start of the Oral Exam, everyone will gather back at the telephone number that you initially called for a closing “circle,” where you will get to say anything you need to say to be complete about the Oral Exam and your Certification process.
