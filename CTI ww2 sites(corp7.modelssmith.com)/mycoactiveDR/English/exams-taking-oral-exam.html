Eligibility for Taking your Oral Exam

Upon completion of the Program, you are eligible to take the Oral Exam. To take it, you must meet the following requirements:

  • 1. Completion of all Homework assignments
  • 2. Completion of all 6 individual Supervisions
  • 3. Completion of 2 Open Topic Calls and the required 4 audio podcasts
  • 4. Completion of your 100 coaching hours and an updated Client Roster
  • 5. Completed, submitted and passed your Written Exam
  • 6. Completed Completion Tracker
