Written Exam

You will receive your Written Exam from your CPL two weeks prior to your last scheduled Pod Call. You must submit your completed exam to your CPL before your last Pod Call.

Click here to download information about preparing for your written exam.

CTI will send your Written Exam results to you via email within 30 days after your final scheduled Pod Call. There is no feedback if you have passed your exam. In the event that you do not pass your Written Exam, you will have the option to review your exam with your CPL and to retake it.

Oral Exam

Scheduling Your Oral Exam

Plan ahead for your Oral Exam, as exam dates are often booked 60+ days in advance. You must complete the Oral Exam within one year of the start date of your Program.

CLICK HERE for more details about scheduling your Oral Exam.

Exam Prep Call Schedule

We strongly recommend that you attend a live Exam Prep Call during the second half of your Program. These calls are led by one of our examiners. They will offer an overview of the exam process and give you some recommendations and suggestions on how to best prepare for the exam. You will also have the opportunity for you to ask any questions you may have about the Oral Exam.

CLICK HERE to download a schedule of “live” exam prep calls and details for calling in. you do not need to register for these calls. if you cannot make any of these calls, you can listen to a previously recorded call (see below).
