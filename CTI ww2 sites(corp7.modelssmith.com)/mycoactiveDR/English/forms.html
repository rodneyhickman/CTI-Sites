Student Forms

Certified Coach Change Use this form if you change your certified coach during your Program. Instructions are included on the form.

Client Release Authorization Use for this form for Client Release Authorization. You should have the signed form in hand prior to recording your client. Electronic signatures are NOT acceptable on these forms. DO NOT alter this form with anything other than the info it asks for or it will be invalid. The original form, with your clients signature, should be kept by the coach and a scanned or faxed copy will need to be submitted to CTI by the end of your Program. PLEASE DO NOT HAVE YOUR CLIENT SUBMIT THIS FORM TO CTI.

Client Roster Use this form for keeping track of each client you are counting for Certification hours.

Documentation for Internal Coaches Fill out this form if you will be using internal clients (people within your workplace) during your Program.
 
Group/Relationship Coaching Use this form to keep track of group or relationship coaching hours during your Program. Instructions are included on the form.
 
Group Supervision Review Use this form with your Group Supervision Calls. Your version does not get submitted to CTI, as your supervisor will be submitting their version of this form. Print out copies of this form to use during your Supervision Calls.

Individual Client Tracking Sheet You can use this form for each of your clients. This form does not get turned in to CTI. However, this format aligns with ICF requirements and will help you prepare information needed for the Total Hours Tracking Sheet that will be submitted to CTI.

Individual Supervision Review Use this form with your Individual Supervisions. This form does not get submitted to CTI. Your supervisors will be submitting their version of the form. Print out copies of this form to use during your Supervision Calls.

Supervisor Contact Sheet This form has the contact info for the Supervisors. Check your Welcome Documents for the names of the two Supervisors whom you were assigned.

Mid-Program Review This is the Mid-Program Review form for you to download, complete and email privately to your CPL.

Supervision Assessment Scale This contains Supervision scoring information and the Supervision Assessment Scale.

Total Hours Tracking Sheet This form is required to submit your required 100 coaching hours to CTI. Submit this form at least 30 days prior to taking your Oral Exam.

Client Game

The Client Game is set up to help you reach your client goals. There are several areas set up to discuss obtaining new clients. Share how you've obtained new clients. Post goals, client leads and get support with accountability.

A shortcut to to the Client Game Discussion Area is found on MY CO-ACTIVE.COM in the Announcements Area. Also check the Discussion Area for a link to a recording of your Client Game Call if you need a refresher along the way.
