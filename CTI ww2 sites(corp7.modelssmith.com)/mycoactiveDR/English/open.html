Open Topic Call Links

Are Your Clients Addicted to Suffering?
Author: Melanie Dewberry-Jones
If your clients are experiencing joy and happiness only as an exception and not as a rule then they may be addicted to suffering. Come, listen and learn how to hear those things our clients use to distract themselves from joy. Once identified you can steer your clients out of their suffering and back to their Fulfillment, Balance and Process.

Coaching (AND) Therapy
Author: Chuck Roppel
Coaching and therapy share common threads. What are they and where do they intersect? How are they alike and different? When are coaches over their heads and when are they right where they need to be? Join this call to learn more about this hot topic.

Coaching Heart and Soul
Author: Virginia Kellogg
Eventually in any coaching practice you begin to attract clients who are already successful and who come to you looking for that something that is still missing. They want more depth, meaning and aliveness in their lives. They want to live based on their heart and soul. This call will explore coaching at the heart and soul level, using language that calls forth change from a deep place.

Coaching the Adult’s Agenda
Author: Leza Danly and Jeanine Mancusi
Part of the coach’s job is to support clients through conflicting motivations and desires to come up with their True Agenda. Motivations of the Child, Adolescent and Ego within are coaching agendas that can never succeed in the present, leading to frustration and stagnation in the coaching relationship. Uncovering and empowering the Adult’s Agenda is the path to real change and Fulfillment. This call explores key distinctions to help you listen for the powerful Adult within each of your clients.

Coaching the Relationship Wedge Don’t Just Talk About It, Coach It!
Author: Marita Fridjhon
All clients show up with relationship issues at work and at home. Are your clients just talking about it? Are they stonewalling, blaming, or defending? In this call you will learn skills to prepare you to coach the relationship issues that show up in your coaching calls.

Completion — How to End a Coaching Relationship with Integrity and Heart
Author: Karen Kimsey-House
Completion is an important part of our day-to-day life. Clean endings are as or more important than joyful, happy beginnings. On this call we will talk about how to complete with integrity and heart, in a way that everyone gets to be heard, regardless of the circumstances of the coaching relationship.

Deep and Wide Change
Author: Steven Filante
We often work with clients who want to jump from one great idea to another, never really landing and making change, never integrating their experiences or discovering what lies under the surface of their wants and desires. This session challenges you to look at your clients and coaching practice from a brand new perspective. Using the CLAIMS model, we will help you move out of problem solving, struggling and suffering with your clients helping your clients find the deeper heart of their lives.

Emotions and Empowerment
Author: Leza Danly and Jeanine Mancusi
How comfortable and confident are you when it comes to responding to the emotions of your client? Are you able to make distinctions between authentic emotions that provide the fuel of empowerment, and the false emotions that create frustration and stuckness? To be a masterful coach, you must first recognize these distinctions. It is only in the vulnerability of authentic emotion that ownership and real change can occur. Join us for a fascinating session on the complexity of emotions!

Failure: From Ouch to Ta-Da!
Author: Grace Flannery
In this call you will learn to be with your client’s failure, with their pain, find the learning, and masterfully reframe the experience to a celebration of the client’s growth and learning.

Geography How Much Fun Can Coaching Be?
Author: Steven Filante
Come play with imagery, voice and body to supercharge the energy of your coaching. Oh and it will be more fun too. For all who don’t really get geography and those who want to be masters.

Handling Financial Realities... with Fun, Pleasure, and Ease
Author: Leslie Lupinsky
How do you coach your clients about money? Finances are often a Saboteur-ridden topic, experienced as a burden to avoid. In this call, you’ll learn tips to combine rigor with pleasure, fun and ease as you coach your clients to financial health.

Holding the Space of Your Client Call
Author: Elaine Jaynes
We so often speak of “holding...” We “hold” our client’s agenda. We “hold the Space” of a classroom, a Pod Call, a client session. What DOES that mean, anyway? This call explores this terminology, and the competency as CTI envisions it and will include brilliant conversation, and some skill drill type practice.

Into the Heart of the Beast!
Author: Jeff Staggs
Where do you find your courage as a coach? How do you tap into your heart, since the root of courage is “cour,” the Old French word for heart? We will explore the structure of fear, where we hold back and how to step into a new place with your clients. Your clients will only go as far as you give yourself permission to go. So how far will you go into your fear?

Intuition and the Body
Author: Cynthia Loy Darst
This call will build on the learning that CTI Certification participants have already had on Level 3, Intuition and Geography, and take the way that they connect and work with a client to a deeper level.

The Magic of Metaphor Turn Up the Transformation Power of Your Coaching
Author: Lori Shook
You've known about the skill of metaphor since Fundamentals. Are you finding that you don't use them or you don't find much potency with them? Perhaps you or your clients throw out a phrase every now and then — “you’re soaring like an eagle” or “I see that you’re stuck in a swamp.” Ho hum. Do you know that there’s a whole rich approach to coaching using mainly metaphors? On this call, you’ll learn how!

Rockin’ Intakes
Author: Jeff Jacobson
Dream what’s possible... then do it! This call will be a wacky, brainstorming-ish topic call where we will discuss together the intake of your dreams, not bound by laws of money or physics. Wild and crazy ideas will be given, and then some participants will be coached on what they would love to create and how to make it happen. We will demolish stale old intakes that bore the client and feel like rote exercises for the coach, and bring instead a livened sense of what is possible.

The Space Place...
Author: Rick Tamlyn
...Coaching’s Final Frontier. This call is about the power of coaching the client around the “space that they are in" versus interacting with the “thing that the client wants fixed.&quot

The Top 3 Relationship Systems... Coaching Skills You Can Do With Your Clients
Author: Marita Fridjhon
Relationship issues frequently are top ranked by individual clients as a primary coaching focus. You may think you are coaching only your individual client, but you really are coaching your client within the context of the relationship system they are bringing to coaching. Discover some of the cutting edge relationship coaching skills that you can use with your individual clients and have them be more effective and fulfilled in the relationships that show up for coaching.

The Voice of Master Coach (How to Use Vocal Tone, Energy and Range in Service of Your Clients)
Author: Cynthia Loy Darst
What kind of impact is your vocal geography having on your clients? Learning to use your voice authentically and effectively is one of the arts of the master coach. In this call you will tune up your awareness of your vocal power and learn how to use it to consciously impact your client and the dynamic energy of your coaching sessions.
