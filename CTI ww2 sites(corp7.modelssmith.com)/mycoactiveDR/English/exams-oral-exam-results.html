Oral Exam Results

Your exam results will be emailed to you within two weeks after your exam date.

Consistently over the past several years, over 90% of our students who take the oral exam are successful and receive their CPCC designation. If for some reason you do not pass the Oral Exam, you may take it again.  To prepare for your retake you will have an opportunity to meet with one of your examiners to debrief the recording of your Oral Exam coaching sessions. Although this debrief is not required, it is highly recommended. 

We request that you retake your exam within 4 months time from your original exam date. PLEASE NOTE: There is a fee to retake the exam.

Beyond your results, there is no feedback if you have passed your exam.
