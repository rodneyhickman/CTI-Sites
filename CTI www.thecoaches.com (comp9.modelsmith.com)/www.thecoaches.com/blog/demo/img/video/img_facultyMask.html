Spotlight on:

The Stake: The Making of Leaders
co-authored by CTI co-founder Henry Kimsey-House and David Skibbins

Today's world requires men and women who can lead from their vision, their passion, and their deepest sense of purpose. They know how to call forth the resourcefulness and creativity of those they lead. They hold a deep belief in their own capacity to change their world for the better. In The Stake, a step-by-step account of how these leaders are created, CTI co-founder Henry Kimsey-House  has teamed up with an award-winning mystery writer. The result is a compelling, readable book about the development of authentic, responsible leaders. TO ORDER YOUR COPY: http://www.thecoaches.com/leadership/resources/books
