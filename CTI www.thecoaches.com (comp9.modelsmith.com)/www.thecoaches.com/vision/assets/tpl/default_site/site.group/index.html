Co-Creating the Co-Active Community Glorious Cause

Welcome Co-Active Community members!! Over the next few weeks, people from all around the world will be joining together to create a shared statement that expresses, the purpose or “glorious cause” of the Co-Active Way in the world. This will be a statement that that we can all share and own together.

We humans have learned and created our world through story. So we have created a fable to act as a story to launch us into the creative conversation. We call this story “The Hedgehog and the Waterfall”. The “Hedgehog” comes from Jim Collins book “Good to Great” and the Waterfall is an image for how we envision the Co-Active Way spreading through the world. We took these two important characters and created a fable that would allow you to move beyond the ordinary and tap into your creativity and heart.

Here’s how it works:

1. Sit back and read, or listen to Karen read, the fable.

2. Answer the two questions that follow.

Congratulations! You’ve done your part.

We will collect all of the words that you send us in your answers and create word clouds with them and then craft a statement that best captures those words and ideas. Then we will reveal what we all created together at the 2014 Co-Active Summit and of course we will share it broadly after the Summit’s completion.

Thank-you in advance for your participation, collaboration and your love.

Karen, Henry and the gang at CTI
