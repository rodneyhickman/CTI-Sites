INTRODUCTION

Welcome to the Co-Active Coaching Bridging Content.

We have updated the curriculum to improve the flow from course to course, to reflect the way our Course Leaders are now delivering the content, and to introduce some new concepts.

The Bridging Content will point out where the differences are between the updated material and your classroom experience. In the Bridging Content, the changes will be highlighted and you will get descriptions and explanations of any new content.

You are expected to complete the Bridging Content as part of your Pre-Work before you attend your next course. The Course Leaders will expect you have completed the Bridging Content and are familiar with the updated content. It is very important to come prepared in order to get the most out of the course.

We are requesting you to acknowledge completion of the Bridging Content after you have reviewed the material.
You will need to click on the button at the end of the course for us to receive notice that you have done the Bridging Content.
The Course Leaders will receive this acknowledgement so they will know you are prepared for the course.

In addition, you will also find links in the content to the Learning Hub which will give you more information and resources on the areas of update.

We are updating the curriculum as part of our ongoing goal to keep developing and evolving. We hope you enjoy the improvements.

Click on Fundamentals to start your Bridging Content.
