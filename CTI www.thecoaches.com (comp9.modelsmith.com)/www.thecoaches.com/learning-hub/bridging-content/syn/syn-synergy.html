SYNERGY & RANGE

The Concept of Synergy
The Co-Active Coach that you are in the world is greater than the sum of all the parts we’ve been working on in these five workshops that make up the Co-Active Coaching Curriculum.
The concept of integration is now a component of Synergy.

» What’s New: Synergy and the Co-Active Model
The synergy of all the parts of the model comes together here, and we see the whole system clearly and see the magic in it.

» What’s New: Synergy and the Coach/Client Relationship
The synergy of the relationship between coach and client is greater than the sum of the two of you. As each leans in and empowers the relationship, that synergy is palpable.

» What’s New: Synergy and the Captain and Crew
The synergy of Captain and Crew is integrated within the client. The whole of this system is much greater than the sum of its parts.

Expanding Range
The Co-Active Coach you are is greater than the sum of all the parts we’ve been working on in the five workshops of the Co-Active Coaching Curriculum.  As all the parts of Synergy are activated the capacity of the coach is increased exponentially.

In Synergy the Three Attributes of Authenticity and Range replace the Four Attributes of Calling Forth. We are focusing on expanding the range of the coach via the Three Attributes of Authenticity and Range:

  • Fierce Courage
  • Aliveness
  • Connection

The four skills below build your capacity to expand your range.

» What’s New: Mindfulness
The energy of Synergy is more mindful (giving full permission, responsibility for impact, authentic reflection, and effectiveness) and less confrontational (“boot camp”, in your face).

» What’s New: Embodiment
Embodiment moves beyond thinking and into living your full authentic presence from head to toe.  You are fully present in your body and have a full range of resources available to you.
When the coach fully embodies the Co-Active coaching role, clients are inspired to live a fully embodied life.  Your coaching becomes organic and intuitive, rather than stilted and rule-based.

» What’s New: Mirroring
Mirroring is not simply a leader/follower exercise. It is coaching in silence. It utilizes the Three Attributes of Authenticity and Range - especially Connection. Beyond words, coaching can be very powerful, and that power is experienced in the body.

» What’s New: Voice
Most of us aren’t aware of our voice and its impact, yet our voice always has an impact. We can use our voice to Evoke Transformation in our client. Playing with the instrument of our voice gives us access to different resources within us and our clients, especially geography and creativity.
