LIFE PURPOSE

What’s New: Fulfillment and Life Purpose

Life Purpose is a new and important part of Fulfillment coaching because, when we are living on purpose we are fulfilled.

Each of us is a unique human being born with a particular Life Purpose. Our life is like a river. We are going to move from one end of it to the other no matter how conscious we are. Connecting with our Life Purpose is a way that we can move powerfully and fully at choice down that river of life. When we get caught in eddies or rapids, we can grab onto our purpose and remember what to do. When faced with a fork in the river of life, we can choose the path of meaning or default to the path of the unlived life. Being connected to our Life Purpose helps us make those choices with courage, compassion, and ease. When we live from default, reacting to our circumstances, life is dissonant. When we are consciously choosing, like navigating by the North Star, Life Purpose helps us steer a course through life that is true to ourselves and resonant. Discovering our true Life Purpose is an ongoing process of excavation, and it is important to continue to refine a Life Purpose statement that is resonant for you.
