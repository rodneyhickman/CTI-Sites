TRANSFORMATION & MODEL MAP

Evoking Transformation
Whereas Calling Forth was something you would do occasionally, Evoking Transformation is something we are always doing one way or another.  Sometimes we evoke transformation through our words, our voice, or our geography.  Sometimes transformation is evoked through our silence or witnessing.   

» What’s New:  Breaking the Rules
Sometimes it is better to break the rules you’ve made up about good coaching for the sake of Evoking Transformation in your client. Evoking Transformation doesn’t come from safe coaching, normal behavior, or default thinking.

» What’s New: Evoking Transformation
“Calling Forth” is one approach to Evoking Transformation.
For more information on Evoking Transformation see Fundamentals Bridge Training.

What’s New: Co-Active Coaching Model Map
In preparation for being out in the world as professional coaches and/or preparing for Certification, it is important that you learn how to effectively evaluate your coaching and the coaching of others. The Co-Active Coaching Model Map below provides support for keeping your Co-Active coaching skills sharp. This is a tool you can use on an ongoing basis to evaluate your coaching skills and see where further development might be needed.
