What’s New: Incremental versus Transformative Change

Co-Active Coaches evoke transformation within their clients. This is not an idea or a technique; it is a commitment on the part of the coach.

Many coaching models focus on Incremental Change. Coaches work with clients to break potential change down into smaller pieces or action steps and clients work step by step toward that end goal. This is not a bad way to go, it’s just not a sustainable method of change by itself.

By working from the commitment to evoke transformation within their clients, Co-Active Coaches use all of their skills and abilities in service of that transformation. Transformation occurs when we shift limiting beliefs, change our perspective, see things in a new light, make powerful conscious choices from the strength of our inner resources that have us living our lives in alignment with our life purpose and values. When a client experiences transformative change, their orientation shifts in a way that nothing is the same from that point on.
