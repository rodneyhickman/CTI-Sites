The Co-Active Model

NEW: The Co-Active Model
We have redesigned the Co-Active Coaching Model to the Co-Active Model. We have found that the Co-Active Way applies across the board to all human beings. This change begins with our foundation, the Four Cornerstones.



NEW: The Four Cornerstones

  • PEOPLE are naturally creative, resourceful and whole.
    By changing this cornerstone from Clients to People, it applies to everyone.
  • Dance in THIS moment. 
    Dance in THE moment felt like a concept of coaching work we do in the future. In reality, all people dance now in the magic of the present moment.
  • Focus on the whole person.
    This cornerstone is not territorial; not focused on who is showing up. It focuses on how we bring all aspects of the whole person into our coaching.
  • Evoke Transformation
    Holding the client’s agenda has changed to Evoke Transformation. Holding the client’s agenda is now a skill we use to evoke transformation. We hold this as follows:
    • To evoke is to call forth.
    • We define transformation as a deep, elemental shift in essence on the path of evolution.

      At its most resonate; life is a journey of growth, evolution and transformation. People are yearning to be called forth into the greatest expression of them possible. They are hoping that someone will believe in them enough to stand for that.  This change puts the coach back in the model.
