    FUNDAMENTALS COURSE WORK

    Before your course

    If you have time before your course, browse through this website and get familiar with the material. 

    Use the left-hand navigation links as your guide.
    Click on Resources to  get access Topics, Tools, and Business Resources.
    Pay special attention to the Professional Ethics section in Topics and the contents of the Business Resources area.

    If for any reason you are unable to complete the preparation and won’t have access to the Learning Hub in the evenings of Day One or Day Two of the course, please plan to do the reading shortly after course completion. 

    During your course

    Set up a client for your practice coaching session on the evening of Day 2 of your course.

    Arrange your life so that you can dive in and have your attention fully on the course and the experience during your 2 ½ days.

    Do your homework

    After your course

    Coaching is a skill and it requires practice. Here are some suggestions on how you can practice before your next course, the Fulfillment course:

    1.     Pick a new competency each day from the COMPETENCY PRACTICE GRID and try it out with colleagues, clients and friends and notice the impact on you and them.

    2.     Enlist two people to become your practice clients. Charge them a minimal fee. Tell them you are training to become a professional coach. We recommend that you have a Discovery Session with them in person to design the alliance and train them in some basic concepts (requests, accountability, Saboteur using your intuition). You may want to do the Wheel Of Life with them. 
    Note: You will learn more about the Discovery Session in the Fulfillment course.

    3.     Hire a personal coach for yourself. There is nothing like being in a coaching relationship yourself to help you understand the benefits of coaching.
