The Differences between Coaching and Therapy

This interview with David Skibbins hits some of the major points on the subject of how coaching differs from therapy. This recording will be useful in examining your own ideas on the topic and can be shared with clients or family members who need help understanding the unique characteristics of coaching.

David Skibbons is a co-founder of MyFullPractice.com, a coaching business which is committed to helping coaches build their coaching businesses. Author of Becoming a Life Coach: A Complete Workbook for Therapists and several other books, David specializes in helping therapists master the marketing, business and coaching skills needed to successfully add coaching to their practice. In addition to his CPCC and status as a CTI faculty member, David is a Psychotherapist, licensed Marriage and Family Counselor and former Clinical Director and Clinical Supervisor.
