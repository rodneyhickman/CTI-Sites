Now There’s an App!

CTI is proud to announce the launch of its smartphone app, Co-Active ThinkPal – powerful coaching tools at your fingertips. The base app is available to download free for iPhone and Android!

Pull out Co-Active ThinkPal when you need a pep talk, mini-coaching session or injection of creativity for you and your team. Deepen learning and forward action with the tools that helped define the field of professional coaching.

Don’t have a smartphone, but still want to know what it’s about? Click here for an offline tour through the features.


Imagine the Many Ways Co-Active ThinkPal Can Help You, Your Clients, and Your Coaching Practice:

A mutual friend introduced Mark to his coach, Charlie. Mark expresses interest in learning more about coaching. Charlie suggests to Mark that he download Co-Active ThinkPal. Following instructions to complete the free Wheel of Life module, Mark emails his responses from the app to Charlie. Together, they go through the visual representation in a sample coaching session. Mark leaves feeling excited about what’s next in coaching now that he is Charlie’s newest client.

Sarah is feeling guilty because yesterday she worked late and missed dinner with her kids. In search of reassurance, she opens up Co-Active ThinkPal. While swiping through the Work-Life Balance questions she lands on one that reminds her she has never missed a bedtime. She feels proud and records a note to self to listen to the next time she feels guilty for being a working mom. 

Will is new to coaching. His coach suggests he download the Co-Active ThinkPal app to deepen learning between sessions. Will listens to the Life Purpose Audio during his morning break at work. In his next coaching session, Will delights his coach by describing how much more creative and resourceful he felt that day. 

Your client, Alex, is a corporate executive. His team’s projects are not being completed in time despite each individual working well. In the next meeting he asks the team questions from the ‘Workplace Communications’ set. As a result of their discussion they all agree the team would be more powerful by checking in more often and together they design creative new systems to keep projects on track. 

Client Emily can’t sleep. She is feeling stuck and overwhelmed. It’s 1am and she can’t call her coach. She picks up Co-Active ThinkPal to sort through her thoughts. Gaining clarity, she emails her ‘notes-to-self’ to her coach. In their next session they hit the ground running; able to dig deeper because of her 1am insights. 

Louise has a life-long friend, Rose, who lives in another city. Feeling disconnected from her dear friend Rose, Louise suggests they become ‘affirmation buddies’ using technology to bridge the physical distance. Each week they pick a new affirmation from Co-Active ThinkPal and share their thoughts about it with each other. Both women benefit from the exchange of ideas and they strengthen their mutually supportive friendship.


Your favorite Co-Active Coaching tools at the touch of a finger:

  • Provocative Coaching Questions to help you and your clients think through the hottest coaching topics
  • Life Assessment Wheels for use in taster sessions and to help gain perspective on the go 
  • Guided Audio Journeys to help you connect with your life purpose and values at any time
  • ‘Notes to Self’ in written or audio form; giving you and your clients the chance to capture inspirational moments and share them between sessions

Download Now

Co-Active ThinkPal is available to download free on the Apple and Android stores. The App comes with a Starter Question Set, a free Wheel of Life and an audio guide about Co-Activity. Further questions, wheels and audios can be downloaded starting from $1.99. 

Whether you are looking for creative ways to enhance your coaching sessions, personal ways to move through problems or helpful tools to move groups and teams to the next level, download Co-Active ThinkPal today.

  

Use Co-Active ThinkPal when you’re stuck in both coaching and in life and share it with your clients to create structure, momentum and connection. Sharing Co-Active ThinkPal can add value to your coaching services without impacting your time or bottom line.

Co-Active ThinkPal has been developed in partnership with The Knowledge Labs, a multimedia production firm founded by a CPCC. A true example of Co-Activity.

The Co-Active ThinkPal base application, which is free to download, contains:

  • Starter question set
  • Wheel of Life
  • Co-Active audio
  • Information about the Co-Active Model

In addition, specialized modules are available individually for in-app purchase:

  • Question Sets
    • Work/Life Balance

    • Workplace Communications

  • Professional Wheel of Life
  • Customizable Wheel of Life
  • Affirmations
  • Homework/Journaling
