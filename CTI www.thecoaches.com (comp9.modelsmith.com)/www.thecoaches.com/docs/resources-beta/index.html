Resources

Welcome to the Resources section of the CTI website. Here you will find support for your coaching practice and your ongoing development as a coach.

Coaching Tools provides a wide range of resources from the CTI Faculty of Coaches covering Co-Active Topics, Applying Co-Active Coaching, and Building Your Coaching Business.

Applying a Coach Approach at Work? Corporate Tools provides resources when working with corporate clients

Books Recommended - CTI has created a list of books that it feels may be useful to you as a student and/or coach. You'll find 4 categories: Coaching, Leadership, Business, and books written by members and former members of the CTI Faculty of Coaches.

Co-Active Network. Once your training with CTI is complete, the impact your training will have on your career, your professionalism and your life is just beginning.

Through our extensive resource network, you can continue to improve your skills, participate in CTI-sponsored lectures and events, keep up to date on new developments in the coaching field and continually interact with other CTI members exchanging ideas and information.

As the jewel of our ongoing commitment to all CTI graduates, CTI has organized the Co-Active Network, a powerful network of professional colleagues and Certified Professional Co-Active® Coaches. This hub of information and resources is available to complement and strengthen your coaching discipline. Members come together to create learning, share resources, cultivate alliances, develop opportunities and build community niches to forward and enhance your lifelong process of human learning, effectiveness and fulfillment.
