Title: The Top 10 Habits of Very Successful and Happy Coaches

The Top 10 Habits of Very Successful and Happy Coaches

Finding it hard to fill your coaching practice these days? Ever wonder if you even have what it takes? Have you also noticed that some coaches just seem to have an easier and more enjoyable time building successful coaching businesses?
It’s not magic and it’s no secret. In this session Steve Mitten MCC will share the Top 10 Habits of Very Successful and Happy Coaches. In 12 years of coaching Steve has spoken with and mentored thousands of coaches around the world, and made a study of just what are the key accelerators to any coach’s success. He is also a student of the wisdom traditions, yoga and positive psychology.
If you are a relatively new coach still working to fill your practice, join us for what will be an enlightening and inspiring conversation on topics that will range from practical practice building actions to personal habits that will attract more success and happiness your way.

Steve Mitten CPCC, MCC, 2005 ICF President, is an award winning, internationally recognized Master Coach that runs a highly successful coaching practice working with leaders, entrepreneurs and independent professionals around the world. He is passionate about what accelerates the growth of human awareness, compassion and effectiveness, and helps his clients find their niche and thrive. Over the years Steve has also made a study of what really works in the building and marketing of a successful coaching practice. You can learn more about Steve at www.acoach4u.com.
