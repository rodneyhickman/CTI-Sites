Tap Your Inner Wisdom:  Free Writing to Empower Your Coaching Practice

Free writing can connect you with your deeper truth. Set a timer for ten minutes and write. Do not stop to think, to edit, or to make the writing neat or coherent. Let your wisdom flow and see what emerges!

 1. Discover your unique brand. Set the timer for ten minutes and use the prompt "I am...?".  See what your writing reveals to you. Read through the writing and underline key words that express your unique brand.

 2. Unearth the benefit of your coaching. Spend ten minutes writing about the value that your clients get from your coaching. Get into your client's perspective. Use this to develop your Hottest Ultimate Benefit statement.

3. Make marketing easier. If you are working on a piece of marketing writing and don't have the right words, stop trying. Use a free write to dump all of your thoughts on the page on the topic. Then go back and highlight pertinent words or phrases that illustrate your point. Use the free write as a place to gather your ideas.

4. Clear the emotional field. If you are struggling with a client and don't have a coach buddy to clear with, set the timer for ten minutes. Begin writing and vent, let your wisdom surface and see what solutions your writing can provide for you.

5. Capture your coaching expertise. After client calls, use a ten minute free write to express any ideas that came up during the coaching. These post-coaching writing sessions are an invaluable way to capture your coaching wisdom. You can use these writings to notice patterns in your coaching and then use the information for marketing materials.

6. Connect with your clients. Do a free write with a client on the phone. Go for three minutes and then read the writing. This is an incredible way to tap into deeper wisdom of the client. You will be amazed at the synchronicities that may arise.

7. Gain confidence. Are you nervous about cold calling? Use a free write before the call to dump all of your concerns. Your wisdom will begin to take over and encourage you to make the call now.

8. Collaborate powerfully. Working with another coach on a project? Do a free write together on your topic and see what your wisdom unearths. Read the free writes aloud and see where you intersect and where you complement each other.

9. Develop programs. Do a free write on your favorite topics. Let your mind wander over the most inspiring presentations that you have given or listened to. Brainstorm topics that you are passionate about.

10. Gather your expertise. Do a free write to explore subjects for articles you can write for your newsletter or other publications. Write as many as you can in ten minutes, then highlight topics that you could begin to write immediately.
