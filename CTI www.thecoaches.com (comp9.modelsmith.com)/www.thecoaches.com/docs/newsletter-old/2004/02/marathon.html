The CALL to Action Marathon- Register Today!

On October 3rd, 2004, 150 coaches and leaders will come together to participate in the Portland Marathon to raise money and awareness for CTI scholarships, Time To Change prison project and other leadership projects. We want you to take a moment to imagine yourself crossing the finish line in Portland. You've done the training to run or walk in this event and now you have completed all 26.5 miles. You've raised over $2,500 from your community of family and friends. And most importantly, you've created a connection to your body and deep sense of well being for yourself.

Join in this first event of the international Co-Active Coaching Community!

You've always wanted a good reason to do a marathon... now you have a great one!

Registering for The CALL to Action Marathon is an act of exceptional courage and commitment. Your may be feeling excitement and at the same time perhaps nervous or even scared. This is normal. That fear will soon be replaced by a sense of exhilaration -- the reward of your marathon experience.

We're here to support you every step of the way. After you register you will be assigned a marathon coach and personal trainer to assist you in meeting your fundraising and training goals. You are not alone. This experience has been designed to be Co-Active... this means that we will be here for each other.

Act Now! Don't let this become something that you meant to do. Visit our website at www.thecallmarathon.com or call toll free 866-909-6040 for more information. For an intimate discussion on why you should do this event call 405-244-4000 box 250 to hear a pre-recorded informational message available to you 24hrs a day.

Join us in the vision of creating a culturally rich and diverse community of Coaches. Where no one who wants to participate in CTI learning will be turned away due to lack of funds. Together, and with your commitment, we can raise over $200,000 for the coaching and leadership community. REGISTER ONLINE TODAY!
