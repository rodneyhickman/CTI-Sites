Article Submission Guidelines

We are currently accepting articles for our newsletter on topics of interest to coaches and building the Co-Active community. Articles should be between 500 and 1000 words in length.   We retain the right to edit articles as needed. All articles submitted will be used at the discretion of CTI.  We do not publish everything that is submitted.  While we do not pay for articles that are published, it is a great way to increase your visibility in the coaching profession. Please send articles (including a short bio that has a way to contact you) for each month's newsletter to newsletter@thecoaches.com by the first of the month. Please also be sure to send a quick note to the same address indicating your interest in writing something so we can plan for your article to be included.
