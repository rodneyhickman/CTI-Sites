On The Road Again

IN THE U.S. ...
In our last printed newsletter we told you that we kicked-off courses in both Providence, RI and Atlanta, GA. Now that we offer the full CTI curriculum in those locations, nearly 400 people have enrolled! We are thrilled to have these new locations available to our fellow Co-Active Coaches on the East Coast.

AND BEYOND ...
CTI will be offering it's full curriculum in the United Kingdom beginning in September 2000. For more information, check out our website or contact UK program directors Penni Blythe (phone: 011 44 1460 61969, email: estrella@hamdon.clara.net) or Linda Taylor (phone: 011 44 1823 664441, email: estrella.taylor@virgin.net).

CTI founder Henry Kimsey-House recently traveled to Japan, where he taught a large group of business executives the principals of Co-Active Coaching. Teaching the course through translators was a bit of a challenge, but with the wonderful support of co-leader Hide Enomoto, CPCC, translators and assistants, Henry Kimsey-House once again witnessed the magic of Co-Active Coaching. "I sat in the room not understanding a single word the participants were saying to each other, yet I recognized the same intimacy, the same connection, the same growth, the same learning and light of comprehension, and the same difference being made that we all have seen as we look out at a room alive with coaching. The work we do translates itself into hearts and souls." This is the first of a series of courses that are planned in Japan.
