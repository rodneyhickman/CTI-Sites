Lauren Powers Interviewed on the J.O.B Radio Show

Working Radio - The J.O.B. is a radio show created by Larry Olson. It is about everything to do with jobs such as finding new ones or getting more out of your current career. The J.O.B. currently airs on 960 AM "The Quake" in San Francisco Bay Area at 5pm.

CTI sponsored 24 weeks of Working Radio broadcasts during the fall of 2008. During that time CTI affiliated speakers were interviewed on the radio show to discuss coaching related topics. The following recordings are the coaching segments only which have been clipped out of the hour-long recordings of each radio show.

October 30, 2008
Lauren Powers chats with Larry Olson about her book, The Trouble with Thinking: The Dangerous Trip from In the Head to Out the Mouth and how our habit of making up negative stories about what is going on around us affects our working and personal relationships and our behavioral choices.

Clip run time: 11:43 minutes
