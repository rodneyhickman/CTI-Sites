Keeping Perspective

Working Radio - The J.O.B. is a radio show created by Larry Olson. It is about everything to do with jobs such as finding new ones or getting more out of your current career. The J.O.B. currently airs on 960 AM "The Quake" in San Francisco Bay Area at 5pm.

CTI sponsored 24 weeks of Working Radio broadcasts during the fall of 2008. During that time CTI affiliated speakers were interviewed on the radio show to discuss coaching related topics. The following recording is the coaching segment only clipped out of the hour-long radio show.

October 8, 2008
Stephanie Collins of JumpSpark Coaching & Consulting shares some thoughts with Larry Olson about staying positive when everyone around you has flown off the deep end.

Clip run time: 9:19 minutes
For more info on 'Working Radio': http://thejob.podbean.com/
