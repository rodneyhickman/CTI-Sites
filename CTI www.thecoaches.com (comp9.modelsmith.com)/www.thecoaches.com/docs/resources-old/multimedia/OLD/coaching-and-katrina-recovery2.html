Coaching and Katrina Recovery

After Hurricane Katrina devastated New Orleans, a group of coaches came together looking for ways to use coaching and the coach approach to make a difference, in a different way. A smaller group of coaches spent the past three years supporting a group of New Orleans community and business leaders in the process of manifesting their vision of building and running a healing center in the heart of the affected region of New Orleans.

Here is a recently released documentary about those coaches and New Orleans community leaders coming together to chart a path forward. For more information on the healing center, please visit http://www.neworleanshealingcenter.org/.
