Books by CTI Faculty

The Business of Coaching

You may be an expert coach, but converting your expertise into a successful coaching practice takes a whole new set of skills. Whether you're considering the possibility of coaching or you already have a practice, this book shows you how to become as good at running your business as you are at coaching. This book provides comprehensive, practical guidance on the essentials of establishing, growing and managing a coaching business. Included in the workbook (on CD) you'll find an abundance of valuable resources, information, tips, techniques, plus activities and exercises to jumpstart your business.
