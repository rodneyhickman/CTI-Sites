Biz Dev Programs

Building a successful coaching business takes time and commitment. CTI values all of our students and graduates and it is our desire to support you and the powerful work you do by providing tools and information to help ensure your continued success.

CTI, in partnership with Business Catalyst Institute™ and Inspired! On Demand™ offers development programs to move your business forward.

Business Development Program

You have all of your credentials. You're ready for clients. Now what?

It's time to focus on growing your business and it doesn't have to be hard. CTI® is dedicated to supporting you and the powerful work you do by providing tools and information to help ensure your success. We value all of our students and graduates and want to see you be successful. That's why we've partnered with two stellar business development programs - Business Catalyst Institute™ and Inspired! On Demand™.

A business development program will help you:

  • Get clear on the uniqueness of what you offer: your branding
  • Create a business founded on your own visions, passions, values and strengths
  • Learn to integrate your own style into enrolling and attracting clients
  • Understand and handle the practical and tactical aspects of business
  • Understand how to effectively manage all elements of your business - enrollment, marketing, finances, budgeting - while still doing what you enjoy most, coaching!

CTI's Business Development Partners

Business Catalyst Institute™

www.businesscatalystinstitute.com

At Business Catalyst Institute™ we know that students arrive with different skills and at varying stages of building a thriving, fulfilling business. One size does not fit all, so we've created our program to work with your needs, whether you are just starting out or wanting to expand. Our courses are offered via small group teleclasses, in short affordable modules that focus on a specific area of business development such as attracting clients, defining your niche, developing your brand, marketing and other business-related topics. The classes offer a wealth of information and tips while also providing personal interaction and support for your journey in the world of entrepreneurship.

We believe in an 'inside-out' approach, meaning that your success is grounded in your own empowered and aware perspective. The more clarity and confidence you have - both as a coach and as a business owner - the more attractive and clear you'll be to your clients and potential clients. This approach is central to all of our courses.

Business Catalyst Institute™ is led by Dorcas Kelley CPCC, PCC, CMC, author of The Business of Coaching, a required or recommended business textbook for multiple coaching schools.

Check out our website for free resources and audio recordings. Come play with us to learn, explore and increase your success!

Inspired! On Demand™

www.inspiredondemand.com/cti/

Imagine having the business resources and inspiration that you need and want - at any point along your path of business success - whether you're becoming an entrepreneur or you're a corporate leader inside an organization. Inspired! On Demand™ presents you with a flexible, tried-and-true program that is self- paced, highly affordable and easy to use.

This program gives you everything you'll need in business from getting grounded in your vision to attracting clients - from developing a financial freedom mindset to practical marketing and branding essentials. Leslie is the co-author (with Joni Mar) of the popular book, The Inspired Business Approach, which offers a fresh new approach for attracting clients authentically and with ease. Inspired! On Demand™ takes the struggle out of business building and puts you in the driver's seat of success. Once you have the basics down, you can join a small, facilitated 'accelerated results team' or attend an in-person class at popular locations around the world.

Leslie Lupinsky, Senior CTI Faculty, CPCC, MCC, has developed this program with Brad Stauffer, CPCC, who is a seasoned branding and marketing expert, publisher and business coach.

Visit our website to receive a free branding e-book and learn more about our self-paced, highly-affordable and easy-to-use business growth program.
