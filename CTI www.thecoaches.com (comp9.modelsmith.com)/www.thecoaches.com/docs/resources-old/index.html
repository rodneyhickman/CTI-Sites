Resource Center

Once your training with CTI is complete, the impact your training will have on your career, your professionalism and your life is just beginning.

Through our extensive resource network, you can continue to improve your skills, participate in CTI-sponsored lectures and events, keep up to date on new developments in the coaching field and continually interact with other CTI members exchanging ideas and information.

As the jewel of our ongoing commitment to all CTI graduates, CTI has organized the Co-active Network, a powerful network of professional colleagues and Certified Professional Co-active® Coaches. This hub of information and resources is available to complement and strengthen your coaching discipline. Members come together to create learning, share resources, cultivate alliances, develop opportunities and build community niches to forward and enhance your lifelong process of human learning, effectiveness and fulfillment.

"I love creating space for people to create a vision of who they really are, at their most powerful. And what they want to do at their most powerful."

Corrina Gordon-Barnes, Co-active Coach
Cambridge, England
