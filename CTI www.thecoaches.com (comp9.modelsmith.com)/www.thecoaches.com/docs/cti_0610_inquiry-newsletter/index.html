No Mail, No Hassle

This is the season of summer travels and the inevitable full email in-box upon return. The Co-active Network doesn’t have to add to your burden. Did you know there is a handy tool inside “Manage Account” that temporarily halts delivery of the Daily Digest from all your Co-active Network communities with a couple mouse clicks?

Yep, there is a feature called “No Mail” to use while you’re away. Simply go to “Manage Account” and select the box “No Mail” and SAVE your changes. I recommend that at the same time you halt Daily Digest delivery, you set yourself a reminder in your calendar to trigger you to reset your account to receive emails again upon your return.

How handy is that tip? All your other automated email deliveries may pile up in your in-box, but you can choose how to control the flow of digest email through the Co-active Network.

Please let me know if there is something you’d like to learn how to do on the Co-Active Network.


Warmly,
Marcia Norris, Co-active Network Liaison
networksupport@thecoaches.com
