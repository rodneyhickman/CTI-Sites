Coaching Careers

Learning the Co-Active Coach training method is your first step to beginning a successfully lucrative coaching career. Becoming a certified coach can impact your career path and life in numerous ways. Your coach training equips you with the skills to:

    » Establish a coaching practice for coaching individuals
    » Become a coaching consultant within large organizations
    » Bring coaching within your current organization
    » Use the relationship skills learned in coach training to improve your existing career

“To me, coaching is about calling people forth into their own significance, so they can realize they’re not just a cog in the machine, but they have a part to play, in the transition happening in the world today.”

Corrina Gordon-Barnes, Co-Active Coach
Cambridge, England
