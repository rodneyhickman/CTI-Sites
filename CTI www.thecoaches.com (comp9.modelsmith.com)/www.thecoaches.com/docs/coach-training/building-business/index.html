Co-Active Sales

How many clients do you want in the next 90 days? 

Set Your Goal and We’ll Help You Reach It 

CTI is offering a dynamic new sales training program that shows you how you can use your Co-Active coaching skills to successfully sell your services to prospective clients and grow your business to the size you want. 

The four seminars will help you:

  • Transform your current perspective on selling by understanding how to apply Co-Active principles to the sales process.
  • Learn to take a stand for what you are worth as a coach. 
  • Learn to prospect for new clients in a fun and profitable way.
  • Use and manage your referral team.
  • Handle “No” and other objections effectively.

And—there’s more! The Co-Active Sales Program is not just another set of content-filled webinars. This program includes a 90-day, small-group accountability process. Nine small-group Accountability calls (led by one of the founders of this sales program) are spread over 90 days to support you in successfully implementing your sales strategy. For three months you will have the support you need to insure that these sales techniques and systems become habits you will use every week to build your practice. By the end of this program you will feel confidant and comfortable both in attracting new clients, and in receiving regular referrals.

Co-Active Marketing (pilot)

Build Your Coaching Marketing Money Machine

Do you want to make more money from your coaching practice? Do you want an ongoing stream of clients that you want to serve, who want, and can afford your coaching? We know you believe in coaching and want to share it with others while making a solid living. The next step in building your “Marketing Money Machine” is to become irresistible to clients out there. That is marketing.

We’ll show you how to follow the lead of successful coaches who engage in ongoing marketing by leveraging your Co-Active coaching skills to attract clients you want to work with, who align with your values, and want what you offer. Let us help you create a marketing system designed to make it easy for you to continually build and nurture the new contacts and relationships who will feed your money machine.

In Co-Active Marketing you will:

  • Define the specific, viable and lucrative target market that your coaching can best serve.
  • Research and tailor your unique approach to this lucrative target market.
  • Create a specific and appropriate range of services (from free to high paying) for your lucrative target market.
  • Build the outreach tools that will reach and impact this specific market of potential clients.
  • Handle “No” and other objections effectively.

In the six 2 hour webinars and the 5 one hour accountability groups you will let go of the beliefs that shut down your money machine: beliefs about what your product is and about who your market is. You will learn the tools and techniques that will allow you to respectfully contact and serve future clients. By the end of this program your marketing money machine will be in full operation or your money back.

Co-Active Executive Coaching

Break into the Executive Suite!

Do you want to move your practice from coaching middle managers up to coaching in the C-Suite? This course prepares you to stand toe-to-toe with the executive facing rapid change, unpredictability, and conflicting demands from all directions.

In this two-day, in-person workshop, you will learn from a former senior executive from a Fortune 100 company who has also been a venture backed CEO to enhance the scope of the coach’s approach to practical, specific, and time-pressured results in the workplace. Hear what it is really like to live the life of an executive and what he or she needs and expects from a coach.

You will learn:

  • The mistakes that will get you kicked out of the office in the first ten minutes, and how to avoid them
  • How to establish credibility without having been an executive
  • How to shift from Co-Active Coaching to mentoring, training, or consulting to enhance your client’s success
  • How to leverage the often intractable circumstance into transformative event that take your client’s leadership to the next level

The value in this program is the direct access you will have to an active, high-level CEO paired with a senior CTI faculty member who focuses on corporate engagements. If you are serious about coaching executives at a high level, this workshop presents a not-to-be-missed opportunity.

Prerequisites:
Completion of core curriculum plus previous experience working in organizations and experience coaching managers or above.

A two-day in-person workshop
