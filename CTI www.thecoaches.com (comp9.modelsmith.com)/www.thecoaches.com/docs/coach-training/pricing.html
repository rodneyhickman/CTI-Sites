Co-Active Fundamentals Risk Free Guarantee

If after attending the entire Fundamentals program, you are not completely satisfied, we will send you a full refund and cancel your course package registration with no penalty. You must have fully completed the course and requested a refund, in writing, within 30 days of course completion. Simply turn in your workbook and name badge to course assistant at the completion of the program with your written request or mail the request within 30 days of course completion.
