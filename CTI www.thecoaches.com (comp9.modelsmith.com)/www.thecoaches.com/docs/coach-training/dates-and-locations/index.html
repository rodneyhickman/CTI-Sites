The road to Certification usually takes 12 months, but for those of you ready for the fast lane, we encourage you to hop aboard this accelerated core curriculum course schedule and get to your goal faster! By taking this Fast Track program you finish the 5 in-person courses in 2 months instead of 5. This saves you 3 months lowering the total time to get certified down to 9 months if you go straight through.

Please keep in mind that our courses build on one another and must be taken in the following order. The Fast Track program requires you to take all of these courses in a group/cohort. CTI reserves the right to register students to a Fast Track series who do not sign up for the full cohort. You can find specific course information in the calendar below.
