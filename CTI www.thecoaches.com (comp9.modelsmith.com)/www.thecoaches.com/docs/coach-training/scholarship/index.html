Scholarship FAQs

    

 

 

Are there any Pre-requisites?

The scholarship program is open to participants who have been recommended by a Program Advisor or Faculty member, who are taking courses in North America and/or the UK, or who are planning to enroll in the Certification program managed by CTI headquarters in the US office. Applicants must have completed Co-Active Coaching Fundamentals to apply. The course tuition for Fundamentals will be paid by the applicant.

What amounts are available?

It will be a committee decision based on the individual applicant, the pool of applicants and the money available in the scholarship fund. We are offering both partial and full scholarships.

Are there any expectations from CTI?

The scholarship participant will be fully engaged with the program and intend on becoming a Certified Co-Active Coach. Faculty members may be called on to provide feedback about participation.

Can I apply even though I am part way through my program?

Yes.

What are the timelines for the application and selection process?

June 16: Qualified students receive an email inviting them to apply.
June 16-July 7: Students submit their applications
July 7: Application Deadline
July 8-23: Public voting on applications videos
July 24-Aug 14: Committee reviews applications
Aug 21: Selected applicants will be announced on social media and students will be notified via email.

How are the recipients chosen?

It will be a CTI Scholarship Committee decision based on the individual applicant, the pool of applicants being reviewed, the votes cast for videos submitted, and the money available in the scholarship fund.

Is this scholarship available globally?

This is open to all participants taking courses in North America and the UK. For international students, you may apply for the Certification program as long as it is the one which is managed by CTI headquarters in the US office.

What is the deadline date for applications?

The application deadline is July 7, 2014.

How long before I know?

By August 21st, selected applicants will be announced and students will be notified.

How do I create a video?

There are many ways to create a video. You can use a smart phone, computer webcam (e.g. Windows Live for PC or Photo Booth for Mac) or a video camera. The device you use to shoot your video will determine the steps needed to upload it onto YouTube. If you are not tech savvy, this is a great time to practice your networking muscle and find a ‘techy’ friend you can ask for help.
