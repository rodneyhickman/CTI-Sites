Freedom from the Saboteur: The Art of Evoking Transformation
with Michele McHall, MA, CPCC, MCC
October 19-20, 2012

In this two-day workshop Michele McHall will guide you through a profound interactive experience in the art of evoking transformation that will put you and your clients on a path to substantial freedom from the saboteur.

You will learn how to bypass the saboteur by tapping more deeply into your naturally, creative, resourceful and whole self. Through this process your Co Active listening ears will go from rabbit ears to satellite dishes as you expand your ability to tune into aspects of your wholeness that get neglected or hijacked by the saboteur. 

Registration Deadline: October 11, 2012

Earn continuing education units from ICF: Core Competencies - 6.25 and Personal Development - 5.00.

The saboteur has the wily ability of turning a thought that may have a 2% truth into a self-defeating story that robs us of our true strengths and convictions. This course will connect you to the creative resources of your Whole IQ or your Spirit, Heart, Mind, and Body intelligences. From an awakened Whole IQ, your saboteur has less power to hold you back from pursuing your dreams. 

Michele intends to inspire insights and offer tools to sustain your liberation from your saboteur so that you can move forward in your own life and coach your clients with greater ease, passion clarity, and power. 

After you register for this course, Michele will send you a questionnaire which you will need to complete and return per instructions at least one week prior to the workshop date. This questionnaire will help you identify the saboteur pattern that you want to transform in the course, and will also help Michele prepare to work with you. Time needed to process the questionnaire is the reason for registration deadline of October 11, 2012.

About Michele McHall
Michele is the author of The Soul’s Dream: A Journey of Intentional Transformation. She has been on the cutting edge of studying and living in the power of creative consciousness all her life. In the last five years, Michele has become a sought-after speaker and facilitator in activating the creative potential of our Whole IQ through her model of Intentional TransformationTM. She has witnessed the phenomenal personal and leadership growth of hundreds of people who are seeking to impact their worlds through greater authentic connection and ease. Michele’s previous career as an actress, combined with her credentials of CPCC, MCC, and an MA in Depth Psychology, make her an inspiring, provocative, and engaging presenter who is bound to turn on your ability to see things in an enlightening way.
