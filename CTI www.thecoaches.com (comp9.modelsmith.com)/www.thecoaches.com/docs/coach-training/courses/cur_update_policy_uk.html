PAYMENT AND ENROLLMENT

You may pay by check, VISA, MasterCard, American Express or Discover. Full payment of the workshop fee must be made at the time of registration. Your electronic signature authorizes CTI to automatically charge your credit card.

BUYER’S RIGHT TO CANCEL

You have a right to cancel your enrollment and receive a refund by delivering a written notice to: The Coaches Training Institute, Hamilton House, Mabledon Place, Bloomsbury, London, WC1H 9BB, United Kingdom.  You do not have the right to cancel by telephoning or e-mailing the school, or by not attending a course you are registered for.

RIGHT OF REFUSAL

CTI reserves the right to refuse a student participation in courses and/or workshops. In addition, upon the recommendation of both CTI Course Leaders, CTI reserves the right to remove from a classroom a student who is negatively impacting other students and/or the learning environment.

REFUND POLICIES

If you choose to cancel your enrollment in a workshop, you will receive a full refund minus a non-refundable registration fee: GBP 47 or Euro 57.

Written notice of cancellation shall take place on the date the letter of cancellation/withdrawal is postmarked, or if hand-delivered, the date delivered to the school. Refunds will be made within 30 days following the receipt of written requests.
