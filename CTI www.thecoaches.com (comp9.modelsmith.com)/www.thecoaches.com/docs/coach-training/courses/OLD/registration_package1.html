Registration

Please make your first and second choice for the courses. If there is no space in your 'First Choice' course you will be placed on a waiting list for your 'First Choice' and, subject to availability, enrolled in your 'Second Choice'. If neither course is available, we will contact you to discuss other options. You will receive written notification of your enrollment status by email.

Your enrollment in a course is based on the receipt of funds for both the deposit and balance due. In the process of enrolling you in your requested course, CTI will notify you if there are insufficient funds. If we do not hear from you within 48 hours of our notification, your requested space in the course will be released. Please wait for confirmation of your enrollment before making travel plans. All course enrollments are subject to availability.
