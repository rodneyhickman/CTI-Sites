FUNDAMENTALS

Welcome! You have embarked on a unique path that will create a noticeable difference in your relationships in the future. CTI is known across the globe for engaging hands-on training. In addition, we know that blending different learning methods, techniques and resources allows different types of learners access to develop their skills.

Please use this blended learning environment to make your learning independent, useful, sustainable and ever growing. Select the activities that best to suit your style, pace and level.

Before your course

Download the Fundamentals section of the course manual by clicking on the photo of the manual cover to the right. If you wish to purchase a printed copy of the entire course manual click here.

If you have time before your course, scan through the manual get familiar with the material. Pay special attention to the areas listed in the Pre-Work below.

Pre-Work (The Pre-Work may be done during the course if you will have access to your manual in the evenings of Day One and Two.)

    » Review Section 3: Business Practices and Section 4: Resources and Tools in preparation for a Q&A on Day 3 of the course.
    » Read completely the Professional Ethics pages in Section 3: Business Practices in preparation for an ethics conversation on Day Three of the course.

If for any reason you are unable to complete the Pre-Work and won’t have access to your manual in the evenings of Days One and Two of the course, please plan to do the reading shortly after course completion.

During your course

Set up a client for your practice coaching session on the evening of Day Two of your course.

Arrange your life so that you can dive in and have your attention fully on the course and the experience during your 2½ days.

Do your homework.

After your course

Coaching is a skill and it requires practice. Here are some suggestions on how you can practice before your Fulfillment course:

  1. Pick a new competency each day from the Competency Practice Sheet and try it out with colleagues, clients and friends and notice the impact on you and them.
     
  2. Enlist two people to become your practice clients. Charge them a minimal fee. Tell them you are training to become a professional coach. We recommend that you have a discovery session with them in person to design the alliance and train them in some basic concepts (requests, accountability, Saboteur using your intuition). You may want to do the wheel of life with them. Note: You will learn more about the discovery session in the Fulfillment course.
     
  3. Hire a personal coach for yourself. There is nothing like being in a coaching relationship yourself to help you understand the benefits of coaching.
