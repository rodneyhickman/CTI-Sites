The Business Builder Virtual Workshop

Building a successful coaching business takes time and commitment... and often a helping hand. CTI has developed our Business Builder program to give you powerful tools and resources to ensure your continued success. You can create the vision, formulate the plan and learn the action steps you need to take to build your business — and, you can do all these things virtually.

If you have a phone and a computer and the desire to build your business, you can participate! Based on the popular Business Builder in-person training, the Business Builder Virtual Workshop will break down the process of building your business into manageable steps.

The Business Builder Virtual Workshop will help you achieve the following benefits:

    » Change the way you view your business — an objective view for achieving better business results
    » Learn from other business owners
    » Implement the most effective sales and marketing strategies
    » Learn exactly where to spend your time and resources
    » Save time. Eliminate uncertainty. Execute the right business strategy now.

The program consists of 10 weekly sessions that are 75 minutes in length. That is 12.5 hours of business training! The price of this program is $575 USD.

Business Builder Expected Outcomes

    » Create a custom-designed marketing plan for your coaching business
    » Develop the skills and tools needed to implement your plan successfully
    » Attract and close new clients quickly
    » Discover strategic strategies to attract visitors to your website, blog, Linkedin and Facebook page

Curriculum Overview

    » Branding You
    » Model your revenue
    » Create new products and services
    » Marketing plan
    » Website strategy plan
    » Website design and development
    » Social networking strategy for blog, Linkedin, Facebook
    » Pricing strategies and offers
