Certification

 

Prerequisites

    » Completion of the first four Co-Active core coaching courses — Fundamentals of Co-Active Coaching, Fulfillment, Balance and Process
    » Have an established coaching relationship with either a Certified Professional Co-Active Coach (CPCC), a Professional Certified Coach (PCC) or a Master Certified Coach (MCC) from the International Coaching Federation

Completing the Application

Click here for instructions on completing the Certification Registration and Application process.

 

 

Introducing the CPCC Logo

We are excited to announce the launch of the Certified Professional Co-Active Coach logo! This new logo is intended to serve you better in your coaching careers, to illustrate your distinguishable skills as a graduate of CTI’s world-renowned coaching curriculum. In our efforts to prevent any ineligible users from wrongly displaying this title, we will offer this logo to you through a password protected area on the website.

Where and how you can get your logo:

    » Please visit http://www.thecoaches.com/resources/partners/cpcc-logo.html
    » You will be prompted to enter an email address and create a password for yourself
    » Click on the link Download CPCC — download should start immediately

Where to use your logo:

    » Signatures, business cards (multimedia and print)
    » Any self-promotional email, website, brochure or newsletter

Usage of CPCC logo requires acceptance of a terms and conditions agreement, which is made available when you visit http://www.thecoaches.com/resources/partners/cpcc-logo.html.
