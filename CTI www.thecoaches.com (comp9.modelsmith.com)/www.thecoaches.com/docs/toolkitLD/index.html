The Co-Active Coaching for Leaders Toolkit provides leaders and managers with tools that enable them to effectively use the Co-Active coaching model and coaching skills to bring forward talent and top performance in the workplace. The toolkit is designed to provide structures to apply concepts of coaching in the actual situations encountered in the workplace.

The tools and resources are organized in four main areas:

Building the Foundation – Relationship

Tools designed to support leaders in establishing a climate in which effective coaching can take place and create the best results. Coaching contributes to vibrant workplaces in organizations where there is a solid foundation of positive relationship. more »

Purpose:

  • Recognize the characteristics of leadership challenges when coaching style is most effective
  • Establish and nurture individual and group relationships that support learning and growth
  • “Walk the talk” of commitment to learning through personal demonstration
  • Reinforce continuous learning
  • Build trust, mutual respect, and honest communication

Success Criteria:

  • High level of trust in workplace
  • High degree of employee engagement and satisfaction
  • Frequent and open developmental conversations with feedback

Bringing out the Best in Others – Learning

Structures that enable leaders to bring forward the best talent in the organization, as well as to have constructive developmental conversations in difficult situations. more »

Purpose:

  • Blending focus on tasks with focus on people
  • Use Co-Active coaching to leverage top talent and move performance from good to great
  • Use Co-Active coaching in difficult performance conversations
  • Support employees in assessing performance and skill
  • Create powerful development plans

Success Criteria:

  • High level of awareness of current and desired performance
  • Frequency and currency of development plans
  • Strong organizational bench strength
  • Productive use of conflict

Purpose:

Success Criteria:
