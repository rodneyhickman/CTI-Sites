Building the Foundation - Relationship

Purpose

A climate of open and positive relationship is the foundation for using Co-Active coaching in a way yields the most powerful results – without strong relationships, efforts to support development in others will accomplish little. A powerful Co-Active relationship is characterized by a mutual investment of the leader and employee (or team) to create a relationship that will most support the growth and performance of the employee (or team). In the diagram below, arrows depict the investment made by both parties, with a return arrow to the employee signifying that the overall focus is on that person’s learning and performance.
