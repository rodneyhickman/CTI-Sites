CTI UK Free Workshop – Evolution in Action

CTI’s free workshop, Evolution in Action, an introductory Leadership Circle workshop, will help you connect your key patterns of action with habits of thought.

Date: Friday, September 9th, 2011
Time: 7:00 - 9:00 pm
Venue: The Hatton, London
Entrance: Free. Places limited to 40.

Pre-registration required: email UKinfo@thecoaches.com

Drawing on the foundational philosophy of CTI’s Leadership Circle Profile Certification Training, in this live interactive workshop, led by CTI leaders Ronnie Clifford and Tony Barton, you will:

  • Identify the predominant Reactive strategy you adopted as a child in order to survive in the world of adults and how unconsciously that same strategy for better or worse continues to shape your identity as a adult.
  • Explore how you have marginalised into the unconscious (shadow) the behaviours you do not consider part of your identity and identify the limitations of this fractured self.
  • Turn courageously and tenderly towards the behaviours lurking in the shadow and claim these behaviours as vibrant parts of the integrated and whole self.
