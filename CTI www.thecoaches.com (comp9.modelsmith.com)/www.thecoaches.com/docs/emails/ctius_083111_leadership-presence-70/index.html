Co-Active Leadership Presence
Discover the power of your natural presence with the Co-Active Leadership Typing System*

Come and take part in a truly revolutionary typing system that will fundamentally change the way you lead. Co-Active® Leadership Presence is an interactive process that identifies and celebrates your unique leadership qualities. This powerful system lets you see and understand the impact you have on other people and how to maximize your effectiveness in any situation.

Most typing systems categorize people into narrow classifications and are based primarily on a left-brain, analytical approach. Co-Active Leadership Presence is designed with a creative, right-brain approach that enables you to embody your natural strengths in a way that you will carry with you for the rest of your life.
