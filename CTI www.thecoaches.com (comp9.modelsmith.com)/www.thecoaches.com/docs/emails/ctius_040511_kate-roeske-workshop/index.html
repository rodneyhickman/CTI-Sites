Co-Active Leadership Presence
Discover the power of your natural presence with the Co-Active Leadership Typing System*

Take part in a truly revolutionary typing system that will fundamentally change the way you lead. Co-Active Leadership Presence is an interactive process that identifies and celebrates your unique leadership qualities. This powerful system lets you see and understand the impact you have on other people and how to maximize your effectiveness in any situation.

Most typing systems categorize people into narrow classifications using a left brain, analytical approach. The Co-Active Leadership Typing System is designed with a creative, right brain approach that enables you to embody your natural strengths in a way that you will carry with you for the rest of your life. Gaining this deep understanding will enable you to step into any situation and create the response that will deliver the results you want.
