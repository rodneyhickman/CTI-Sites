New Introductory Course

Manchester | 26-28 February 2010

CTI’s Introductory Coach Training Course – ‘The Fundamentals’ – comes to Manchester, UK, 26 – 28 February 2010.

After 10 years of presenting our leading coach training courses exclusively in London, from February 2010, we will be adding Manchester as a new venue, to make it easier for people in the North of England and Scotland to access our training.

To celebrate this move, we are offering a special promotional price of just £275 (+VAT) on our inaugural introductory coach training course, The Fundamentals - a saving of £100 on the normal price of £375 (+VAT).

If you’ve ever been curious about a career in coaching, or how coaching skills can help improve your ability to communicate and form effective working relationships then at £275 (+VAT), our Manchester Fundamentals course represents the perfect place to jump in.

The Fundamentals is by far the best way of experiencing the rigorous coaching model, the thorough content and the deeply experiential learning style, which has made Co-active Coaching so successful, enjoyed by over 20,000 managers, consultants and new career seekers worldwide.

On this 2 and a half day non residential course nothing is held back. Course participants get the opportunity to explore Co-active Coaching in a small group where a focus on active participation and live demonstration, offers extensive opportunities to coach and to receive feedback on their coaching.
