Dear Co-Active Community,

I love the phrase from Systems work—“myth change”. This is when the story of a person or small cluster within a group changes completely. The Global Co-Active Summit™ was a myth change for Co-Active in the world. As a member of the Co-Active community you are a part of that unfolding story and your participation matters.

In a post Summit blog entry I spoke about some of what we learned and committed ourselves to and also promised much more to come. We want everyone in the Co-Active community to be part of the important conversation that began at the Summit. Whether you were physically present or not, we want you to feel connected to the whole and to the conversation.

We have created a DVD which allows you to (re)experience the opening and closing ceremonies and the keynote speeches of Kevin Cashman and Lynne Twist. This DVD gives everyone a common touchpoint as we stand shoulder to shoulder in our commitment to creating a new dream for our world.

Because we feel the message of the Summit is so important and relevant to everyone in the Co-Active community, we are offering the DVD at a low price ($12.00 USD plus shipping) that simply covers our production costs.

ViaTech, one of our Co-Active Summit sponsors will be providing ordering, payment, and fulfillment services on our behalf. Available in both SD DVD (North America) and SD PAL (Europe) formats. Click here to buy.

Over the coming months we will also be publishing content related to the Co-Active Summit in an archive on the Co-Active Summit website. Look for the “Archive 2011” button to access it and return often to discover more.

Much love and gratitude,
