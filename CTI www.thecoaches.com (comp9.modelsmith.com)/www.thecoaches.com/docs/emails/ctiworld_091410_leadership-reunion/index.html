Reconnect with Your Tribe at the Co-active® Summit

Through the mist, you can hear the drumbeat...calling to your heart...calling you to gather at the first Global Co-active® Summit in Marco Island, Florida, February 24-26, 2011. For more information on the Summit or to register, go to http://www.CoactiveSummit.com/.

Prior to the Summit, I’ll be gathering with my Tribe; the incredible and brilliant people who lead CTI’s programs. I encourage you to do the same! Spend a few days before or after the Summit gathering with your own Leadership Tribe and then attend the Summit together (and/or roommate together). You’ll have an opportunity to reconnect to your own Tribe and also be inspired by the larger tribe of Co-active leaders and coaches from around the world.

The venue is completely stunning and a perfect place for you to gather. We took particular care to find a location close to the beauty of nature and at the same time comfortable and nourishing. The special Summit room rate is available for 3 days before the Summit and 3 days after. Book directly with the Marco Island Marriott either by calling (800) 438-4373 or click here to book online using our special group code. Be sure to book your room early; this special group rate is on a space-available basis only.

This Summit will be a transformative event for everyone attending, designed and co-created in true Co-active style, unlike any large group gathering that you have ever attended. You’ll have a chance to learn and work with a wide range of visionary Co-active Leaders from fabulous keynote speakers to your favorite CTI Course Leaders and select graduates of the Co-active Leadership Program.

Your presence is important and needed. The Co-active Summit is the opening of something new, an opportunity to re-invent ourselves individually and collectively. For more information on the Summit or to register, go to http://www.CoactiveSummit.com/.

Whether we have worked together or not, met in person or not, you are an important part of CTI and of “Co-active in the world.” I send you love and gratitude. I look forward to seeing you in person in February and to learning, growing, evolving, transforming...together.
