It is that time of the year for the Annual CTI Certified Professional Co-Active Coach program survey. All coaches who were students of the Certification Program are invited to complete the survey.

The online survey may be found at: http://www.zoomerang.com/Survey/WEB22B33A385DR. You may also copy and paste the link into your web browser.

Please take a moment to complete our online questionnaire. We are interested in the coaching services you provided during the 2009 CALENDAR YEAR.

We appreciate your response by Friday, September 30, 2010.

The Coaches Training Institute (CTI) informs prospective students about coaching as a viable profession and provides data about how our graduates are using their coaching skills and CPCC credential.

As a requirement of licensure with the California Bureau for Private Postsecondary Education (BPPE), CTI collects this information annually from our Certification program students and graduates. We also publish the results in our annual "Disclosure Statement", which may be viewed on our website.

Additionally, we use the information to learn more about you and to improve our programs. Please be assured that names and individual responses are kept strictly confidential.

Thank you for participating in our survey!
