The Heart of Successful Coaching: A.I.R.
What is it that makes coaching such a unique way of engaging with people? Wherever we have been trained, whatever our models, truly great coaching provides a dynamic and humanistic approach that orients around three foundational abilities:

  • Authenticity: The ability to live our lives from the inside out rather than the outside in.
  • Intimacy: The ability to form deep connection with a diverse range of people, some who are very different from ourselves.
  • Response-ability: The ability to engage creatively with the circumstances of our lives—that is, to respond to what is needed in the moment rather than from fixed and lifeless protocols.

In this insightful and entertaining evening, Karen Kimsey-House, co-founder of The Coaches Training Institute will share insights from her 32+ years in the coaching industry.

The Co-active Relationship:
Four Keys to Transformative Conversations
We each care deeply about the people in our lives. We yearn to be great leaders, managers, coaches, partners, parents. So often, however, our communication has a very different impact than the one we intend. The ability to engage in dynamic, alive and empowering relationship is a key factor for success in every area of our lives. This “relationship agility” is not so much a function of highly developed skills. Instead it rises up from a foundational orientation to both ourselves and others. How do we shift our mindset so that we can connect more deeply and engage in conversations that are truly transformative?

This workshop will be an interactive blend of context and experience in a unique and dynamic learning environment. Participants will the leave with a whole new framework for who they need to be in the conversation of their life.

Relevant for coaches, managers, leaders, parents and anyone wanting to have a transformational impact on the people they work with, lead and love.

In addition to serving as CTI’s CEO, Karen is co-author of the industry best seller, Co-Active Coaching and a well loved workshop leader, keynote speaker and coach.
