Atlanta Co-Active Community Event on August 12th

The Coaches Training Institute invites you and your guests to join us for our second community gathering. This is an opportunity to introduce your friends, colleagues and family members to the power of CTI’s Co-Active® Coaching up close and personal. Join us for a not-to-be-missed evening of fun and learning.

Why venture out?

  • Mix and mingle with your CTI community while enjoying tea, cookies, conversation, and fun.
  • Experience engaging Co-Active Coaching activities with Senior Leader Kelly Jones Waller and community leaders! How is Co-Active transforming you? How are you evolving to transform others?
  • Chances to win! Attending CTI alumni AND guests will be entered to win a 45-minute complimentary coaching session with Kelly Jones or a copy of the book Co-Active Coaching, signed by Karen Kimsey-House and MORE!
  • Every attending guest receives a voucher for a complimentary coaching session with a Certified Professional Co-Active Coach.
  • Attending guests who register for Atlanta Fundamentals Fall 2011 will receive a $200 discount AND a copy of the book Co-Active Coaching signed by Karen Kimsey-House AND a $200 discount on the Intermediate Series!

What’s Your Piece?

This event is the perfect opportunity to learn more about Co-Active Coaching, connect with your local Co-Active Community, and hear how Co-Active Coaches are evolving and transforming the Greater Community! Have a client success story to share? Call Tammy with your stories and community contribution.
