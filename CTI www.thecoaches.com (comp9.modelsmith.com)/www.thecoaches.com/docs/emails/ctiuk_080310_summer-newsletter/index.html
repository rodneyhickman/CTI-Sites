Leadership Circle Profile Training – London October 19-21

See Your Client’s Opportunities for Development Rise to the Surface

The Leadership Circle Profile Certification is a three-day experience resulting from extensive collaboration between CTI and The Leadership Circle. This training program, which combines data and theory of a world class assessment tool with the Co-active® Coaching model, is designed to prepare you to successfully use The Leadership Circle Profile, The Leadership Culture Survey, and the Follow-Through Tool. This program is approved by ICF for CCEUs. Registration deadline October 4th. To sign up for our next introductory webinar please click here.

 

Fast Track – Accelerate Your Journey To Certification. September 17th – October 28th

CTI’s Fast Track program comes to Europe for the first time this autumn.

CTI’s Fast Track Co-active® Coach Training Program is designed to prepare Co-active® Coaching students for our Certification Program over a shorter program schedule, just over 8 weeks, although with the same rigorous coach training curriculum and number of ICF accredited hours, undertaken on our regular program.

If you want to expedite the start-up of your coaching practice, minimize travel time and expense, and bring new skills to your job quickly, then Fast Track may be the program for you.

Please contact or refer your friends, colleagues and clients to Judy Rich judy@thecoaches.com or +44 (0)845 299 8199.

September 21st: Peaceathlon – 24 Hour Global Conversation for Peace

Run by one of CTI’s first ever graduates in Europe, Penni Blythe-Jones, the Peaceathlon will be held on the UN International Day of Peace this September.

Run as a series of 24 back-to-back, one-hour telephone conversations focusing on and inspiring an experience of Peace, with a link from the previous hour and to the following. Our CTI community will be well represented through Hosts who are leading some of the hours. Co-Activity is core to engaging each and every participant in a powerful experience.

If you are a CTI Leadership Graduate and interested in hosting one of the hours, or want more information please contact Penni at: +44 (0) 1669 650 467 or penni@centre4creativechange.co.uk.
