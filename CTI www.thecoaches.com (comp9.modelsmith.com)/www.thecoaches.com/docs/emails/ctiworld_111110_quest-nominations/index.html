We Invite You to Participate in Quest Showcase Nominations

Dear Leadership Program graduates,
As part of the Co-active Summit™ experience, we want to showcase some exceptional leader quests in our exhibit area that inspire, exhilarate, or challenge. It is up to YOU to nominate the quests that you want to see showcased.

To nominate your favorite quest, please collect the four pieces of information listed below and send it to coactivesummit@thecoaches.com. Deadline for nominations is November 30, 2010.

Nomination email must include:

  1. Your name, phone number, and email address
  2. Name, phone number, and email address of Leadership grad who created the Quest you are nominating
  3. Title of the quest you are nominating
  4. Up to 200 words describing the goals, activities and outcomes of the quest and what moves you to nominate them

Note: Please nominate freely. Once nominations close, CTI will follow up with nominees to see if they want to be showcased at the Summit. Quest exhibitors will be required to follow through on specific action items in order to participate. Details will be supplied to nominees during the selection process.

Five in a Tribe
CTI will upgrade your group from the Standard to Premium ticket if Five in a Tribe (or more) attend the Co-active Summit together.

The Five in a Tribe offer works like this:

  1. Coordinate among yourselves and choose one spokesperson.
  2. All attending tribe mates register online through http://www.coactivesummit.com/.
  3. Once all in your tribe have registered, the spokesperson should email (a) your tribe name and (b) a complete list of attendee names to coactivesummit@thecoaches.com.
  4. Once our Customer Service Representatives have upgraded all the tickets for your group, the reps will send a confirmation back to the spokesperson.
  5. The spokesperson will then be responsible to communicate this information back to the group.

Why Summit? Think back to your first Co-active experience and how much it transformed you, your coaching and leadership skills, and your clients. Watch a related inspirational video with Henry Kimsey-House.

Everything about the 2011 Co-active Summit is designed to help you relive that experience…and to transform all over again. Whether it’s our highly experiential exercises, dozens of creative learning pathway choices, or one of our motivating presentations, you will come away energized, inspired, and connected.
