The Story of Transformation
Crafting the stories we tell about ourselves, our relationships, and our world

We are built to experience our world through stories. When we understand the basis for our narratives, we can use stories as pathways to reshape our world and our place in it.

Through interactive exercises, we will:

  • Discover techniques for exploring and crafting stories as tools for transformation
  • Explore some of the emerging science that explains why humans are storytelling animals
  • Understand why our stories look and feel the way they do
  • Learn new ways to understand ourselves and others by discovering the meaning of the stories we tell
