When Change Is Hard

 

Is change ever easy? It comes at us these days like a speedball—fast and furious. It makes it pretty difficult for us to step up to make the changes we want to make.

We all want and need to change from time to time. Many of you are in the change business, working with people to negotiate waters filled with sharks and uncertainty. Even if you coach change you need to do change yourself sometimes, too.

Often change only requires a minor tweak. Maybe you just need to nip a product or price. Other times, you’re looking at mega-scale shifts. Perhaps you need to figure out how you’re going to conquer your disinterest in selling your services.
