If you are a:

  • professional who recognises better communication is the key to career advancement
  • leader committed to bringing out the best in your employees and teams
  • learning professional whose position includes providing formal or informal coaching

Then this course is for you. In this two-and-a-half-day course, nothing is held back.

Course participants get the opportunity to explore the transformative power of Co-active® Coaching in a small group, where the focus is on active participants and practical experience rather than lecture.

Over the weekend, participants will learn:

  • listening techniques to expand awareness and improve communication
  • powerful questions to empower and inspire action
  • intentional relationship design to get the most out of any working relationship
  • how to expand leadership presence for greatest impact as a coach and beyond

By the end of the program, participants will have concrete tools to immediately improve their effectiveness, as a well as a clear idea of how to incorporate coaching into their life and work.
