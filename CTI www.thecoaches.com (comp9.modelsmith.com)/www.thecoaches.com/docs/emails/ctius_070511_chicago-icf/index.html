Join CCF for 10th Anniversary Celebration

In support of the Chicago Coach Federation’s milestone celebration we’re reaching out to all Co-Active coaches in the Midwest. The CCF has put together a dynamic program with multiple events over the weekend of July 15-17, 2011.

CCF has asked our community to help spread the word. Coaches from all Midwestern ICF local chapters are welcome. Meet with your colleagues and benefit from the expertise of ICF Global President Ed Modell and Cynthia Loy Darst who was named “One of the Top Ten Most Influential Coaches in the U.S.”

At this decade point in their existence, The Chicago Coaching Federation has set itself a goal to become a prime resource for continuing coaching education as well as a rich coaching community for the entire region. If you are within driving distance of Chicago we encourage you to take this rare opportunity to experience high caliber learning and conversation in your own “neighborhood.”

CTI is proud to point out that Cynthia Loy Darst, a CTI Senior Faculty member, will be a major part of the CCF 10th year kick off celebration. On Friday, Cynthia will be the Keynote Speaker for the celebration. On Saturday Cynthia will lead a powerful workshop. On Sunday Cynthia will provide personalized coaching feedback for coaches looking to grow.

FRIDAY, JULY 15, 2011
Honoring the Past, Celebrating the Future
Speakers: Ed Modell and Cynthia Loy Darst
Time: 5:00 pm– 9:00 pm
Cost: $50.00 per person (Hors d’oeuvres to be served. Cash Bar.)

SATURDAY, JULY 16, 2011
The Mighty Metaphor - A Workshop for All Coaches
Speakers: Cynthia Loy Darst
Time: 5:00 pm– 9:00 pm
Cost: $200.00
CCEUs: 5 in Core Competencies Category

SUNDAY, JULY 17, 2011
The “Art of Coaching” Master Class
Speakers: Cynthia Loy Darst
Time: 10:00 am – 4:00 pm
Cost: $475.00
CCEUs: 5 in Core Competencies Category

For detailed information about activities, locations, costs and earning CCEUs associated with the Chicago Coach Federation’s Tenth Anniversary Celebration, please visit chicagocoachfederation.org.

PS – On Thursday, July 14, 2011 from 6:30-8:30 p.m., CTI will be hosting a pre-celebration reception in Rosemont with Cynthia Loy Darst to celebrate and share Co-Activity with your friends and colleagues. The reception is open to CTI students and alumni and their guests who are interested in learning more about Co-Active® coaching. The July 14 Co-Active Reception hosted by CTI is free so please pre-register yourself and your guests before Tuesday, July 12th by emailing registration@thecoaches.com.
