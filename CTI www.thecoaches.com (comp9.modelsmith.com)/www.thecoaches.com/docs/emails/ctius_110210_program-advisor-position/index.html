CTI Is Seeking a Program Advisor

CTI® is the industry leader in Coach Training, setting the standard for ICF certified coaches around the world. As the creators of Co-active Coach® training, CTI brings a unique perspective and training process to the coaching industry. Person to person experiential learning coupled with the Co-active philosophy has revolutionized the lives and careers of thousands of managers, leaders and coaches throughout the world. The Co-active model has one clear mantra: through the power of relationships, we can change the world.

We are currently seeking a Program Advisor. This position is a consultative sales position responsible for guiding students through the registration process and being sure that potential students find their correct path. Our Program Advisors are often the first CTI voice our students hear and therefore play a key role in our relationship with our students. A pleasant disposition and a strong understanding and appreciation of CTI’s values, as well as the ability to handle multiple functions are key requirements. Other qualities and characteristics needed for success include: the ability to understand the essence of CTI’s work; curiosity; high emotional intelligence quotient; accountability; creativity; relationship agility; adaptability.

The successful candidate must have strong relationship management and communication skills. It is preferred that the individual has sales experience in the consulting or training industry. This position reports directly to CTI’s Internal Sales Manager.

Interested candidates should email resumes to humanresources@thecoaches.com.
