The Co-Active Relationship: Four Keys to Transformative Conversations

We each care deeply about the people in our lives. We yearn to be great leaders, managers, coaches, partners, parents. So often, however, our communication has a very different impact than the one we intend. The ability to engage in dynamic, alive and empowering relationship is a key factor for success in every area of our lives. This “relationship agility” is not so much a function of highly developed skills. Instead it rises up from a foundational orientation to both ourselves and others. How do we shift our mindset so that we can connect more deeply and engage in conversations that are truly transformative?

This workshop will be an interactive blend of context and experience in a unique and dynamic learning environment. Participants will leave with a whole new framework for who they need to be in the conversation of their life.

Relevant for coaches, managers, leaders, parents and anyone wanting to have a transformational impact on the people they work with, lead and love.
