Event Announcements

The Co-active Summit™. February 24-26, 2011, Marco Island, Florida, USA.

Our collective Co-active desire is to effect sustainable change in the world in new and bolder ways. And we need you. Join us for the first-ever global Co-active Summit.

This highly experiential Summit is constructed to give everyone who attends a unique pathway on a contextual journey that we all share in common. You’ll connect with other community members, learn new tools for success, and walk away inspired and ready to help in the unfolding story of Co-active Coaching and Leadership. Click here to watch CTI co-founder Karen Kimsey-House speak about the summit, right now.

  • 23 in-depth pathway presentations led by leaders in the coaching profession and carefully designed to help you transform yourself and your coaching business.
  • 2 powerful keynote speakers: Lynne Twist and Kevin Cashman. These two global thought leaders are partnering with our event design team to help create a unique and groundbreaking experience at the Co-active Summit.

Each participatory activity created for the Summit is steeped in the same context and builds on the last, effectively coordinating your journey with all the other journeys being taken.

There’s also news of the forthcoming Co-active Summit in Florida, where the Co-active community will come together to explore the role of Co-activity in creating deep transformation in the world.

Already, over 300 participants have signed up, including more than 75 CTI faculty members. Will you join them? Find out more www.coactivesummit.com

European Co-active Coaching Community Day
Friday, 6th May 2011 – Authentic Impact

The ECCC invites you to join other co-active coaches on for a day of learning, exploration and networking. The day will include a series of empowering workshops that will help you improve your coaching skills and grow yourself as a coach.

Content confirmed so far...

  • Advanced Coaching SkillsIn this interactive workshop, you will learn advanced coaching skills that have been developed to promote non-profit leaders to make the biggest difference possible around the world – led by Dave Ellis
  • Evolution in Action: Embracing Your Shadow exploring 'who you are as coach' and the impact that has on your coaching and how you are able to serve your clients – led by Nick Martin and Ronnie Clifford

Along with lots of other interesting and valuable learning and content. Plus the usual networking and fun with other co-active coaches!

All this and more on Friday, 6th May 2011. Click here for more information.
