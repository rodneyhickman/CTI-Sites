Dear CTI Boston Community:

Thank you for the FANTASTIC attendance at our first CTI Boston Mix & Mingle Event. The blizzard held off and we successfully kicked off the New Year in CTI style!

CTI Leader, Art Shirk, did an incredible job of providing information for the CTI alum and their “new-to-coaching” guests.
