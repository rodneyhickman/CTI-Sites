Letter from the CEO

On February 24-26, 400 men and women from 22 different countries around the globe gathered at the first global Co-active Summit in Marco Island, FL. We came to be in community, to learn together and to chart a new course for ourselves and for Co-activity in the world.

As we gathered together at the Co-active Summit we tasked ourselves with representing our larger Co-active Community. As the Co-active Summit unfolded, it became clear that these 400 people representing 22 different countries and our larger Co-active Community stood firmly for a shared vision. We saw that we were collectively committed to stepping into the fire of transformation with fierce love and courageous, committed action for the sake of changing the dream of the world...for the sake of creating a world that works for everyone.

I love Co-active coaching. Over the years, it’s been one of the great joys of my life to watch this beautiful profession bloom and grow. I am passionate about Co-active Leadership. I think our approach is unique and powerful.

At the same time, it has been clear for a while that Co-active coaching and Co-active Leadership are vehicles for something larger...a particular WAY of being in relationship with each other. A Co-active Way of living and working together that touches every aspect of our lives.

The Summit was a solid confirmation of what I have sensed for some time...that our work in the world is shared and that we are connected to each other through a collective dream for humanity.

We are not alone. There are many co-collaborators who are playing for global change and Co-activity is but one part of a large and committed orchestra. At the Summit, we were privileged to hear Kevin Cashman and Lynne Twist share their visions for global change. We heard about a new grassroots movement called Four Years. Go. which is committed to creating a shift in human consciousness within the next 4 years. Click here for information on Four Years. Go.

As we move forward from the Co-active Summit, it is clear to me that we have taken a firm stand for the transformative nature of our work and our emerging and shared commitment to work for change.

However you engage with what you have learned at CTI, you are a part of that vision making itself real in the world. Day by day and conversation by conversation, we are working together to create a shift. Every moment and every relationship counts...with our loved ones, our children, our co-workers our managers and absolutely with our clients.

Thank you for being a part of my work and my life.

Love,
KKH

Karen Kimsey-House
CEO and Co-founder
