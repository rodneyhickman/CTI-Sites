Happy Spring Everyone!

In training coaches over the past 15 or so years, one of the things I notice that they struggle with the most is acknowledgement. It’s so…INTIMATE…so real. And I notice that this is one area that coaches particularly want to get RIGHT.

I find myself deeply touched by the struggle. It’s my sense that we want to get this acknowledgment thing right because it matters so much, because we care so deeply about empowering and nourishing our clients. So, I thought I would write a short piece about acknowledgement for this issue of our newsletter. I hope you find it helpful.

To begin, I want to acknowledge YOU for your heart in the matter, for the way in which you care so deeply about your clients and for your shining desire to serve and nourish the people in your life. How do I know this about you when we may have never even met face to face! Because that is one of the biggest reasons that people are drawn to coaching and to CTI in particular. So thank you for your willingness to open your heart. It takes a great deal of courage to love in this world and I believe that love is the most healing thing we humans have to offer and share…. Co-Active Network Tech Tips

By Marcia Norris

Fall into Community

Several juicy conversations came up in the Main Community discussion area this summer—full of astute observations and passionate wordsmithing. But activity is picking up all over the Co-Active Network for Fall. Our beloved volunteer Community Leaders are busy planning new fare. The variety in Network groups allows colleagues to gather virtually to go deep into special interest topics or meet other coaches in the ‘neighborhood.’

Did you know there are 36 Niche and 25 Geographic communities on the Co-Active Network? If you have an idea for a new community or feel called to volunteer your leadership talents to a new or existing community, please send a message to networksupport@thecoaches.com. Your involvement helps create a stronger and more vibrant community which serves to support and champion Co-Activity, coaching and leadership to achieve great impact in the world.

Check into Calendar Events

In the Main Community space there is a calendar where public events sponsored by the breadth of Co-Active Network Communities are collated. A quick look around proves that Network members are exploring a wide range of ideas in interesting ways. You should join the Career Coaching Community event Speak & Write to Attract Clients for a panel discussion on September 25. The featured panelists will share how they’ve built their coaching practices through activities such as publishing articles, blogging, public speaking, and networking.

The Women as Leaders Community recently hosted a call titled Awaken the Leader Within on September 23. The wise women who lead this group turned the typical approach on its head. Instead of starting at the beginning and imparting new information and experiences, this group began at the end—with what people already know. Click on the topic title to listen to a recording of that discussion.

In June the Creative Artists Community discussed the classic book Art and Fear, but on September 18 that community tweaked the theme and explore Art and Love. Hmmm, that’s an intriguing pair of topics under discussion by a group of very creative thinkers. Click on the topic title to listen to the discussions via recording.

Main Community Event

The Co-Active Network Support Team is planning a (roughly) quarterly series of Main Community Events starting with the topic How To Build A Coaching Information Business on September 29, 2008. Please join Ford R. Myers, M.Ed., President of Career Potential, LLC, as he hosts a dynamic and interactive discussion of techniques for building a thriving business.

Would you like to recommend a well-known speaker or expert for a future Main Community Event? Is there a topic you think would make for an interesting community call discussion? Please send your ideas and suggestions to networksupport@thecoaches.com. What's Happening in the HUB

Do you know about the Co-Active Hub?

Building a successful coaching business takes time and commitment. CTI values all of its students and graduates, and it is our desire to support you and the powerful work you do by providing tools and information to help ensure your continued success. The HUB includes programs and organizations that CTI has joined in order to benefit our student body. CTI continues to negotiate discounts for our graduates with these programs and organizations. The HUB also includes time-sensitive deadlines and offers, so be sure to come back often! You will find useful information and tools you can use today, including:

  • Business Development Tools
  • Coaching Tools
  • Co-Active Network
  • News & Events
  • CPCC Cafe

Don’t miss the Coaching Tools section! You will find both our personal and professional Assessment Wheels in this section. The assessments contain areas that, together, represent one way of describing a whole life or career. The Wheels measures your level of satisfaction and range of expression in these areas as a snapshot in time.
