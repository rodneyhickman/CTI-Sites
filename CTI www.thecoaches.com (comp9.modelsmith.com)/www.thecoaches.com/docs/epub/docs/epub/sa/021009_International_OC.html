The Coaches Training Institute Wants to Celebrate
International Coaching Week with You!

In recognition of International Coaching Week, the Orange County Chapter of the International Coach Federation (ICF-OC), with CTI as a GOLD SPONSOR, will be offering an event to showcase the power and benefits of coaching on Friday, February 13, 2009.

Keynote speakers will be John Gray, Ph.D., the world's foremost relationship and gender expert and author of Men Are From Mars, Women Are From Venus and his new bestseller, Why Mars & Venus Collide, as well as Jim Cathcart, one of the most recognized professional speakers in the world and author of the bestseller Relationship Selling and number two best-selling e-book The Acorn Principle.

Throughout the day, there will be morning and afternoon presentations on a variety of coaching topics and opportunities to be coached.

Whether you are currently part of the coaching community, considering coaching as a profession, or exploring the possibility of working with a coach, you will benefit from attending this exciting, dynamic day.

Attendance will be limited to 300 participants and it is suggested that you register early, as last year's event sold out. Mark your calendar and participate on Friday, February 13, 2009, from 8:00 a.m. to 5:10 p.m., at the DoubleTree Hotel Santa Ana/Orange County Airport.
