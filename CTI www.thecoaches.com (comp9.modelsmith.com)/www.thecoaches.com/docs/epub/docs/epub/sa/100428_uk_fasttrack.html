Hop on Board CTI's Accelerated Core Curriculum Course This Autumn

 

This October, CTI will be launching its Fast Track Coach Training in the UK, enabling new career seekers to begin CTI's esteemed certification process in just over eight weeks -- when normally it takes up to six months.

The Fast Track Coach Training program is also suited to those who prefer a more intensive learning style, or for managers eager to unlock their employees' full potential right now.

However, when we say Fast Track, we don't mean hasty.

The duration and depth of the course material remains the same as our regular five-month coach training program, the Core Curriculum.

Course participants still undertake 104 ICF-accredited hours of live, experiential coach training over five sequential learning modules, covering all of the ICF's 11 vital coaching core competencies.*

All course participants get the chance to coach and be coached themselves, as well as to learn powerful communication tools they can apply straight away in their careers and personal lives.
