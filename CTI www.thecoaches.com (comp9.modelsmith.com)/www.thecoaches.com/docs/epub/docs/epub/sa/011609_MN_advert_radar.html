February 6-8, 2009 Fundamentals in Minneapolis, MN

CTI's two-and-a-half-day Co-Active Fundamentals course is the first course in our Co-Active™ Coaching curriculum. Whether you are interested in using coaching skills in your current profession, considering a career in coaching or just wanting to improve your communication with the important people in your life, Co-Active Fundamentals has something for you.

Our dynamically interactive hands-on training provides a powerful introduction to the techniques and skills of Co-Active Coaching. You receive numerous opportunities to coach and receive feedback on your newly acquired coaching skills. By the course end, you will have a very clear idea of how coaching can fit into your life. If you choose to register for the remaining course series--Fulfillment, Balance Process and Synergy--you will receive FREE comprehensive business development services from Inspired on Demand™ to support your journey in becoming a successful, professional coach. Your package includes:

  • Five illustrated e-workbooks to jumpstart your coaching practice

  • The Co-Active Coaching book, 2nd edition--complete with a Coach's Toolkit, questionnaires, checklists, forms and audio CD--all free.

To qualify, you must register no later than seven days after completion of the Fundamentals course, or sign up for the entire package now for more savings!
