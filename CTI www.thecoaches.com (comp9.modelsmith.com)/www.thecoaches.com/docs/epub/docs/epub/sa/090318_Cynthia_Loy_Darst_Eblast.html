Save the Date! Pass on to your friends in the S.F. Bay Area!

We invite you to join us on April 8, for an evening of spark and learning as we explore with Cynthia Loy Darst, MCC, how to meet and utilize "Your Inside Team."

Her provocative and experiential session will introduce you to a way to coach your clients in their relationships with themselves. You will learn a new way to work with a stuck client, and gain new access to and awareness of your actions. By the way, if you have done work with sub-personalities (Voice Dialogue, TA, NLP, Lucid Living, Jungian Chair work, etc.), this workshop will offer you new coaching approaches to integrate with them. If you have never heard of these things, no problem, this will be a fun and easy introduction!

This session is designed specifically for experienced coaches--newer coaches will gain a lot from this in-depth interactive experience.

Please register at sfcoaches.org for this event and review the attached invitation above for more information.
