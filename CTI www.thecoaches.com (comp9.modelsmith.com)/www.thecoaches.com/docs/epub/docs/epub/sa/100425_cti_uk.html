CTI UK | Summer Update

Dear CTI Friend:

We are very aware of the key role our senior graduates play in the Co-active community and so want to keep you informed of new developments at CTI, so you can communicate what’s available, when you feel called to do so. In other words, we want, where possible for you to know first and not have to wait for the email newsletter to find out what we’re up to.

So, today I’m writing to let you about two new initiatives.

i.) The first is simple. For the next two Fundamentals courses (May and July) in London, we have reduced the price to £275 (+VAT). If you have any friends, clients or colleagues who are curious about Co-active coaching, do let them know.

ii.) This second is that this September we will be launching our new Fast Track Core Curriculum coach training in the UK.

We’ve had great success in the US with the Fast Track program, which allows course participants to cover the Fundamentals and four modules of the Core Curriculum in about eight weeks, compared with the five to six months it normally takes.

Although this represents a new direction for how we make our coach training available, the duration and depth of the course material remains the same as the courses that you originally undertook.

The only difference to the format is the duration, so people who prefer a more intensive learning environment, or who already know they want to be a coach, can have an accelerated opportunity to reach their goal.

So, if you know anyone, interested in Co-active Coaching, who would be more suited to the Fast Track format, then again please let them know. www.coaching-courses.com

If you have any queries about the above, or anything CTI is up to in the UK, then don’t hesitate to contact me directly. We’d love to hear from you.

Thank you for your time and continuing support.
