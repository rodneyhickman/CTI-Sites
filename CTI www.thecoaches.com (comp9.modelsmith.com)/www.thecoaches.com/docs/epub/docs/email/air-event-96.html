Creating Co-Active Culture: Changing Business, Transforming Lives – an evening with Karen Kimsey-House, CEO & Co-founder, The Coaches Training Institute

While our culture’s emphasis on performance has yielded some remarkable achievements, many people feel something is missing…Why is there a nagging feeling of emptiness and disconnection from themselves, their loved ones and their colleagues? Why is it a struggle to stay passionate about work? It’s easy to lose sight of the value of real connection, intimacy, self-awareness, listening deeply to and nurturing others – the very qualities that inspire what we do, restore us and give our lives fulfilling meaning.

Please join Karen Kimsey-House for an evening learning about how AIR – Authenticity, Intimacy and Response-Ability – can help bring more aliveness to every part of your life, including the workplace. As the CEO and Co-founder of The Coaches Training Institute, she has worked for the past 25 years to bring relationship agility to bear inside many organizations. She is the co-author of the best-selling Co-Active Coaching, now in its third edition with 100,000 copies in print in 10 languages.
