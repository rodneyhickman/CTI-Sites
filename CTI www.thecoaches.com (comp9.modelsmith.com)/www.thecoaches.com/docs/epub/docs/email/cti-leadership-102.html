Always been curious about the CTI Leadership Program?

Have you or someone you know ever considered participating in the Co-Active Leadership Program yet hesitated to enroll due to concerns about the time commitment or the financial cost? Gain a deeper understanding of yourself and your path in your world or community. Break through self-perceived limits and gain confidence that comes from knowing yourself. Do this work in a community of like-minded peers who will hold you to your true magnificence even when you can’t see it yourself. Discover new ways to be in relationship in all areas of your life.
