Co-Active Leadership Graduates:

CTI's pioneering innovations in coaching and leadership have resulted from a continual loop of piloting new cutting-edge ideas and learning from participants' reactions and feedback. This process enables CTI's founders to continue to innovate and pilot new ideas. As participants in these pilots, you get to experience the latest creations of these industry visionaries and play a role in shaping our industry's future.

Retreat 5: Leadership Mastery is a new addition to the Leadership curriculum that we're piloting this year. The first pilot was held in March 2005 to great reviews. The second pilot will be offered July 12–17, 2005. If you're ready to take your leadership to a new level, come join us for this cutting-edge program.

About the program:
Retreat 5 begins where Retreat 4 leaves off and moves your leadership forward by taking you to new heights of Level 4 leadership: full permission and full responsibility for impact. You will learn a new technology for more accurately reading and interacting with "Level 3" (no kidding!), with lots of opportunity for Level 4 leadership and feedback. Some of the skills you will hone during this retreat include deepening of Full Permission and taking Responsibility for Impact; big stretches in Co-Leading; an unforgettable experience of Creating from Everything; and you'll have lots of FUN (of course)!
