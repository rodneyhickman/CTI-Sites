First Year of CTI Polska

“Amazing grace…how sweet the sound”
by Paweł Sopkowski

At the start, let us thank all the contributors of the first year of CTI Polska. Special thanks to Lori Shook, for her energy, real commitment and concern; all the leaders, for their openness for new, special Polish culture; the CTI partners, for welcoming us warmly and supporting on our way; and clients, for deciding to trust us. There are many more people that we are grateful to and not enough space to list all the names.

One more special thank you to Kasia, our office manager, for all her hard work, persistence and passion. Thank you, Kasia.

This was a wonderful year (well, we know it is not a full year, so what?). We have run nine courses up until now. The first group of 20 people has finished the whole program. It was like a miracle, a privilege, a grace to be able to see all the students experiencing the strength coming out of the Co-Active way of creating and maintaining relationships—the relationships full of acceptance, respect, sincere interest, concern, and servant-minded.

Every time we run a course it feels like an “amazing grace.” Poland and Polish people have suffered a lot in the last 250 years of history: wars, Nazi occupation, Communism. At the courses, we can feel it. There is such a level of pain, suffering and fear.

At the same time, people help each other, coach each other, respect each other, accept and trust each other in an amazing way. The students have overcome their limitations, dared to dream, dared to be themselves. It is such a privilege to witness the butterflies coming out of their cocoons. Wonderful. Amazing. Beautiful. People.

Thank God for this opportunity. Thank you all for all the heart and support.

We feel better and bigger things are on the way.

  • We are organising the first Coaching Congress in Poland on the 12th of October.
  • We run monthly coaching master classes—three-hour workshops for existing coaches.
  • We are developing the first group of Polish supervisors.
  • We are going to launch Polish Co-Active Network on August 31.
  • We are eager to run the first ORSC Fundamentals in late autumn.
  • We are going to implement a special loan program for our students—$6,000 USD for all five courses is a fortune in Poland.
  • We want to open a Polish Coaching Portal—www.4coaching.pl.

On the more wide – social arena - we will:

  • Run a specially designed training program for a group of high school teachers.
  • Continue to have a scholarship program that provides coaching education to the people that cannot afford it—pastors, family counsellors, hospital therapists.
  • Support the efforts of ICF Poland and Christian Coaching Network of Poland.
  • Continue to explore possibilities of running a coaching program for prisoners.

We need you, your ideas, wisdom, thoughts, prayers. Stay with us.

Blessings from Poland,

Paweł Sopkowski
Managing Partner
Coaching Center (licencee of CTI)
