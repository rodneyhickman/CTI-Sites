Spring International Co-Creative Coaching Studio Day!

I first heard about The Co-Creative Coaching Studio over the CTI Yahoo group when Carol Ross posted this wonderful model she was using to bring coaching to the world. I was immediately inspired and jumped at the chance to work with her. Since then, Carol, Patricia Michel and I have created The Co-Creative Coaching Studio niche community, which was unveiled November 2004 and now—less than two months later—has more 60 members in the United States and abroad.

As coaches, we know that our work can change the world, so I figured why not get some group energy going and see what kind of broader impact we can make. With this in mind, I put forth a challenge that coaches from all over the world come together and create Co-Creative Coaching Studios on January 8, 2005. Several coaches have signed up to take this challenge.

Now I want to expand the challenge: Let's all welcome Spring 2005 by holding a Co-Creative Coaching Studio on Saturday, April 2, 2005. This date gives us plenty of lead time for finding a location, pulling together three coaches, putting out events listings in local papers and chatting on the niche to get all the support you need to make the Spring International Co-Creative Coaching Studio Day a huge success. Let's intentionally coach people all over the world simultaneously and feel the ripple effect we can have when people are impacted by the power of Co-Activity.

If you have not joined The Co-Creative Coaching Studio niche community, please join us today. And don't forget to list your Co-Creative Coaching Studio on The Co-Creative Coaching Studio niche community calendar, where our whole community can see it!
