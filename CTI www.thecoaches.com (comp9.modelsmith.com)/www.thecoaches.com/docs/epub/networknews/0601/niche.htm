Introducing MF3 Coaching: The Masculine, Feminine, Third Way Coaching Niche

We hold that re-claiming our ever-evolving masculine and feminine energies is a pathway to wholeness. The more we evoke and express the full range of masculine and feminine energies, the more we experience and express our core selves. This full experience and expression contributes to our personal evolution, as well as to the healing and growth of the world.

The energy of the feminine and the energy of the masculine are infused in everything, from negative and positive electrically charged sub-atomic particles to individual men and women, to whole corporate cultures, not to mention countries and continents.
What is possible when we fully honor the core masculine and feminine energies within ourselves and others?

This is a cross-gender niche. All of us have at our disposal the verdant energies of the masculine and the feminine, regardless of gender. Each of these energies brings with it gifts that are wanted—and needed—in the world. Paradoxically, we often operate from “preferred,” or societally “appropriate,” aspects of these energies while suppressing others.

When we speak of the “Third Way,” we are pointing toward combining or working with these energies in a fashion that embraces and celebrates the dynamic tension between them. By developing awareness and deeper consciousness of both masculine and feminine energies, our clients experience a fuller sense of themselves and gain access to a wider range of self-expression.

The "Third Way" is not a specific place or a fixed way of being. It is the capacity to both inhabit and transcend the duality of the masculine and feminine. It is the capacity to embrace and live from the center of paradox with consciousness and enjoyment.

Where do we begin? If we are going to look through a “Third Way” lens while coaching clients, we need to explore this path for ourselves. We need to ask some questions! For instance, how does one consciously tap into the life force that lives at the center of the co-creative dance between masculine and feminine energies? What are the unconscious—and conscious—assumptions we hold as individuals and in the collective about the feminine and masculine? What impact do those assumptions have on our relationships—with ourselves and with others? How do we experience core feminine and/or masculine energies? What are the many aspects of these energies? Many of us have limited our expression or suppression of the masculine and feminine to sexuality; what other expressions of these powerful energies are available to us? How do they live within us? How do they live between us?

We begin to get to know and unleash the true form of these evolving energies by becoming deeply curious.

Part of our stake is that our very existence as a species counts on us waking up to new possibilities and acting on them. Consider that the way our internal masculine and feminine energies relate to one another is a microcosm of how we experience and impact the world. One path to wholeness and peace—personally and for our world—is through each of us embracing the unique dance of our own inner feminine and masculine energies, continually finding and evolving the Third Way that lives at the center of this dynamic paradox.

In this niche community, we are building a foundation on which to bring the work (and PLAY!) of this delicious dance into the world. We invite men and women alike to join us as we traverse the terrain of masculine and feminine energies and learn to dance.

There is great power to be tapped within the feminine and masculine energies that live within and between us. It’s time to play and explore! Will you join us?
