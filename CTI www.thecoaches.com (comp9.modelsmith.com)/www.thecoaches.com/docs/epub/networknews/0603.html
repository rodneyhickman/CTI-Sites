Artist as Leader

We hold our clients naturally creative, resourceful and whole, right?

“Naturally creative.” What does that mean? Both words have their roots in the same meaning. “Nature” comes from nasci, meaning “to be born.” “Create” comes from creare, meaning “to make, produce, arise, grow.”

In practical terms, it means that creativity isn’t reserved for the artist. In fact, creativity is your natural state of being. You’re creating every second of every day, even when you don’t feel it. You need only look at your nighttime dreams for proof: what could be more creative than to turn off your body and mind and still make a fantastic movie, even if you’re the only audience member.

 Network Support Wizardry

Hi Everyone,

Let’s dive into some particles, aka the “doing” of the Co-Active Network.

In service of creating fun and ease, I have created a…

New policy for the PROMOs thread: I have decided (with great feedback and collaboration) to change what constitutes a PROMO on the Co-Active Network.

ANY services or products that are offered on the Co-Active Network, free of charge or for a fee, belong in the PROMOs thread. Do not start a new “promo or promos” thread. Go to the existing thread and post from there. Some examples of promotional posts would be: Free Info Call, I Need Clients for Certification, I Build Websites, I Have Free ‘Stuff’ For You, Sign Up for…you get the idea. We’ll learn as we go. Thank you to everyone who posts so diligently to the thread. Am I missing anything?

For now, the only exception I see, is course participants looking for homework clients. These can still be posted separately.

Responding to a discussion can create an unintended impact. For example, all the “add me to the list” replies end up going to 6,000+ people. If someone is compiling a list of interested members, please send the originator an email separately.

Thread evolution is a wonderful thing! Sometimes the subject of a thread takes on an entirely different topic. Are you sure you want to post to that thread? Are you creating confusion? Inquiry.

Quick Help is your best friend. I often get questions on how to do this or that. Most of these inquiries are covered in the Quick Help area. This link is located in the upper right-hand corner in every page’s links header. Need to stop emails? Want to post a message and just don’t know how? Need to change your email address or password? Want to read about the functionality of the site? Click on Quick Help and then if you get stuck, email me at networksupport@thecoaches.com.

 Ask Judith

Dear Judith:

I just got a new client who has been a successful CEO for the past 20 years, and at the age of 65, he has decided to retire and start a new chapter of his life. I’ve never worked with a retired person before. Do you have any suggestions for places to focus our work?
—R.E..

Dear R.E.:
Retirement is a specific rite of passage whose meaning has shifted a great deal in the last century. Due to medical, social and personal advances, most people who reach the traditional age of retirement are in good mental and physical health and have many productive years ahead of them. Many Baby Boomers have had successful work lives and thus have the means to enjoy a fulfilling retirement. It is likely that many people who have the financial ability will opt for voluntary early retirement, such as your client has chosen.
