Katrina: The Stories We Tell Ourselves Matter

Outside of a coaching context, where we go for the bottom line, our stories really do matter.  Stories reveal what we believe about ourselves and each other.  They can create connection and inspiration in the midst of disaster and a changing world.  It is through stories that we create what we want to see in the world.

And so, we want to tell you a few of the stories from our own Co-Active community of people who have felt the call to get involved in being of service to the survivors of Katrina.  These are stories of synchronicity, of stepping forward, and of being in the right place at the right time.  They show how connected we are as a Co-Active community and they are laying the groundwork for what is to come.

Turkey Co-Active Coaches Community

Turkey Co-Active Coaches Community is one of CTI’s 20 geographic communities, with 29 members. In total, three ”The Fundamentals of Co-Active Coaching” courses took place in May 2004, October 2004 and June 2005 in Istanbul, Turkey. About 60 people attended these courses.

One Core Curriculum course has been completed and 16 participants from the first two Fundamentals courses completed the “Synergy” course in June 2005. Of these 16 pioneer Co-Active coaches, 80% have chosen coaching as their main profession. Wow! This figure is higher than we expected.

Yin-Yang You

Writing has taken a back seat lately. Life has been busy, alive and, in some ways, quite settled. I write best when turmoil stirs in my heart, sometimes for myself, others times for others. This week has been such a week of celebration, full of such activity and people that I haven't had time for much reflection. When I have, my mood and thoughts have been high and clear. Winter is coming and with it comes the never-ending rain. Maybe that will be when the dark-side will bring us some new light. I suppose some might wonder what that means.

The dark side: we all have one. Think about it, all those things we don't like about ourselves. Bad habits, behaviors, attitudes we've been told are not nice; many things make up our dark side. Too often it is suppressed, not to be recognized or embraced. Rather than admit it is there, we would prefer to say we don't suffer from any of these afflictions and suppress the negative aspect. Here's the rub: what you deny owns you, what you accept gives you back your power. Deny it and what you resist will persist. The irony is that the whole consists of both dark and light.

I thought I’d give you a quickie tech column this month, as I’ve had a lot of inquiries with regards to starting a community here on the Co-Active Network.

In the spirit of Co-Activity, I look for at least two to hold and lead a community—individuals with depth in the niche they want to represent or folks that have a passion to grow their local region and be the relationship guides for their coaching community.

Simply go to the Main Community home page and click “Resources.” There, you will see a link for the applications for either a Niche or Geographic community. Be sure and fill them out and send them to networksupport@thecoaches.com. I’m happy to review them to see if we’re a match!
