As a professional coach, you do a lot of things to market yourself and build your business. While it is essential for you to be bringing in new clients, how about making sure that you keep the clients that you already have!? Excellent coaches retain clients and get lots of referrals. That means a lot less energy spent looking for new clients and a lot more time spent coaching!

As you grow in experience and competence, you gain strength and confidence in your coaching. And, along the way, it is normal to fall into some traps and bad habits. If these habits go unchecked, your client roster will start to thin, it will get more challenging to keep and attract clients, and, worst of all, you will start getting bored. Yikes!

Join Cynthia Loy Darst, MCC, for a day of experiential fun and irreverent challenge, guaranteed to arouse your creativity and get you painlessly out of your traps!

Cynthia Loy Darst, MCC
Passionate about raising the bar on quality in the profession of coaching, one of the first to gain the designation of MCC from the International Coach Federation (ICF), Darst was a founding member of the PPCA and the ICF. She was one of the first leaders for both CTI and the Center for Right Relationship.

A favorite in the coaching world, Darst delivered "Celebration, Metaphor and Magic" to a standing room only crowd at the 2005 San Jose ICF conference and delighted her coaching colleagues in 2006 at the St. Louis ICF conference with her demonstration of MCC level coaching.

Cynthia is a passionate pioneer in the world of professional coaching. Since 1994, she has trained thousands of coaches through The Coaches Training Institute and continues to work with coaches at the PCC and MCC levels to grow their artistry and mastery as coaches.
