Quest...leadership begins here.

Are you considering the Co-Active Leadership program? Not quite ready to commit? Take a look at Quest; it is the perfect opportunity to engage in the heart of CTI's offerings, especially if you have been considering Co-Active Leadership.

Designed to give participants a safe place to find or reconnect with their inner leader, Quest is a stepping stone on the path toward Leadership. Get to the heart of your true purpose and move forward with confidence and grace by addressing the fundamental questions: Who am I?   Where am I going?   How do I get there?

Take that first step, register for Quest today! If you decide to continue your journey toward Co-Active Leadership and register for that program within 12 months of your Quest course, your entire Quest tuition will be applied toward your Leadership course fee!

If you choose to continue on the path after Quest, Leadership will foster your potential and skills through the development of an international community of peers and leaders who grow and learn together through a variety of shared experiences. Start with Quest, continue with Co-Active Leadership.
