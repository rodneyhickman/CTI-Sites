Cool New Tool!

CTI has an exciting new feature on its website: The Assessment Wheel. This is a tool that contains areas that, viewed together, represent one way of describing a whole life. The tool measures your level of satisfaction and range of expression in these areas as a snapshot in time. As you work through the Assessment Wheel, you will discover areas in which you can acknowledge yourself for the success you have created and areas in which you may want to improve your level of satisfaction. Take your turn at the Assessment Wheel!

Print versions of the personal and professional Assessment Wheels are available for download. You can also post the Assessment Wheels on your own site! Learn more.
