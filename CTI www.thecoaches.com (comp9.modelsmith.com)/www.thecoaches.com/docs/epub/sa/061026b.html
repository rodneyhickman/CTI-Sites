Job Description
Marketing Project Manager

Overview
The Coaches Training Institute (CTI) has an exciting opportunity for a strategic marketing professional to join our team. Reporting to the Vice President-Marketing, the Marketing Project Manager is responsible for the strategic design, successful implementation and evaluation of key marketing programs across web, print, event and direct media. This is a leadership role in a growing company, working closely with external advertising, promotion and publicity partners, and CTI staff in marketing and other departments. 

Strategic insight, goal orientation, initiative, creativity, team leadership, excellent written and oral communication skills and ability to manage multiple projects are required for success in this dynamic role.

Education and Experience
The successful candidate will have a relevant post-secondary degree, plus four to six years of demonstrated ability in the following areas:

  • strategic marketing program design, as well as marketing plan implementation
  • setting and achieving project goals
  • project team leadership
  • innovation in project design and implementation
  • working effectively with external partners and cross-functional internal teams
  • market analysis and project evaluation
  • superior verbal and written communication skills
  • experience working in advertising, promotion and publicity in web, print, direct and event channels

Additional Information
The position is full-time, based in San Rafael, CA. Benefits include medical, dental, vision, 401K, FSA, life and disability insurance.

Compensation
Compensation is commensurate with skills and experience.

About CTI
CTI is one of the world’s leading coach training and leadership development organizations, working with individuals and organizations in North America, Europe, the Middle East and Far East. Our culture is one of entrepreneurialism, inclusion, respect, creativity and empowerment for everyone in the organization. We are committed to being a great place to work, and to making a big difference in the world.
