Cool New Tool!

CTI has an exciting new feature on its website: The Assessment Wheel. These are tools that contain areas that, viewed together, represent one way of describing a whole life. The tools measure your level of satisfaction and range of expression in these areas as a snapshot in time. As you work through the Assessment Wheels, you will discover areas in which you can acknowledge yourself for the success you have created and areas in which you may want to improve your level of satisfaction. Take your turn at the Assessment Wheels!
