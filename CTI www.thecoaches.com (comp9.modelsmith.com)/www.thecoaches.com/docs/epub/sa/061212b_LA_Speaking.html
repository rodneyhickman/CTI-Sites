Wednesday, December 13, 2006
6-8:30pm

Did you know that Eric Kohner and Cynthia Loy-Darst are speaking at the ICFLA's Holiday Dinner Meeting/Party tomorrow night?

Eric and Cynthia will be working with Co-Active coaching skills through the lens of the holidays. They will be building on the world of "foundation," explored in November. Join them and take a step further into exploration, ah-ha moments, new perspectives and a chance to try some things out.

They have promised to play with us, laugh with us and give us something to take home, something we can reflect on and bring to our work in 2007.

It promises to be a content-rich, playful event like only Eric and Cynthia can create…a perfect end to this year!

The challenge is on to use our imaginations and dress for this holiday soiree. A very special prize will be awarded for the person with the most festive attire! There will even be a “California Santa Claus.”

We look forward to seeing you at this very special holiday event.

Thank you for being part of our coaching community!
