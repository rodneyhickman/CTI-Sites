What is The Leadership Circle Profile?

The Leadership Circle Profile (TLCP) is a breakthrough among 360-degree feedback instruments. It is the first to connect a well-researched battery of competencies with the deep motivations and underlying habits of thought. It reveals the relationship between patterns of action (doing) and the internal assumptions that drive behavior (being). TLCP integrates all this information in a 360-degree feedback instrument that brings key issues to the surface instantly. The data in TLCP reveals itself in seconds. At a glance, the client's whole life (inner and outer) is accessible. It immediately gets your client in touch with what is working, what is not and why -- and sets the stage for a profound coaching conversation. Learn more.
