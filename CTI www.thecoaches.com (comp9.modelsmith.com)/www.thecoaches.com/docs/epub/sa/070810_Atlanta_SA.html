About Fundamentals
CTI’s Fundamentals program provides training and practical experience in the techniques and skills of the coaching profession, specifically Co-Active Coaching. This course is oriented toward participation and practical experience rather than lecture. You will have extensive opportunities to coach and to receive feedback on your coaching.

As a result of taking this course, you will learn to:

  • Use the four cornerstones of Co-Active Coaching with your clients.
  • Communicate with your clients using the five contexts of Co-Active Coaching.
  • Use the life (balance) wheel with clients.
  • Distinguish and use the three levels of listening.
  • Demonstrate the most-often-used coaching skills.
