My Dear Ones,

Hello! I'm excited to be talking to you about a new project. We want to learn about your lives, interests, and aspirations. Your feedback will help CTI provide you with high quality programs and services. We plan to field several surveys, each with a different focus, over the next several months.

Survey 1: General Demographics:
http://www.zoomerang.com/Survey/?p=WEB229ARXHUAUD
Survey closes on July 3, 2009

In order to avoid inundating our community members with survey requests during this process, any individual will only receive one personal request from us by email to complete a survey. However, if you wish to participate in multiple surveys as we move through our research topics, please check the Co-active Network for future survey links.

To thank you for responding to our surveys, respondents to one or more of our survey sections will be eligible for a gift drawing of an iPod touch.

All surveys are confidential and will be used for internal purposes only. No personally identifiable information will be seen by anyone at CTI or its students. A third-party research company will collect all data and report on the aggregate level.


Thank you for participating,
Karen Kimsey-House
President, CTI
