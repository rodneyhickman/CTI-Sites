An Introduction to:
Co-Active Coaching

The Coaches Training Institute cordially invites you to attend our seminar: An Introduction to Co-Active Coaching. Join us and learn how to use The Co-Active Coaching Model as a catalyst for positive change in yourself and your organization.

Who Should Attend
If you are a manger, internal coach, or curious about becoming an internal or professional coach, and want to make a more positive impact on people, you should attend this seminar.

Session Descriptions
Please select the session that best ﬁts your needs.

Morning Session
8:30AM to 11:00AM
The morning session of An Introduction to Co-Active Coaching, designed speciﬁcally for executives, managers, and coaches within an organization, demonstrates how to build a solid foundation of Co-Active Coaching skills for professionals to apply in their roles within their company.

Evening Session
7:00PM to 9:30PM
The evening session of An Introduction to Co-Active Coaching, designed for individuals interested in becoming professional coaches, demonstrate how The Co-Active Coaching Model provides practical training and the necessary skills to become a professional coach.
