Hello Fellow Co-Active Coaches,

I would like to invite each of you to attend this year’s ICF International Conference in San Jose, California! If you haven’t been to an ICF conference, or haven’t been for a while, this is the one to attend. (And take note that the discounted early-bird registration rate is still available through to August 31.) It will be an event to remember as hundreds of coaches from around the world have spent months developing this conference to make it an extra-special event—being our 10th anniversary—and ensure its value to individual coaches and the coaching community.

The ICF Conference provides an amazing opportunity for coaches to meet face-to-face and talk with many other new and experienced coaches from around the world. With so much time spent connecting via phone or e-mail, I think you will find a special energy and value in the opportunity to connect in person. (I love the learning, connections, inspiration and plain old fun of these events. There is something very special that happens when you get more than 1,000 coaches together. I always come away inspired with some great new ideas to grow my business.)

In San Jose, there will be presentations that speak to exactly where you are in your coaching business. There will be many opportunities to grow as coaches. Presenters will address the issues and concerns faced by new coaches to assist them in developing skills to build profitable and productive businesses. Other presenters will challenge long-time, established coaches with mind-expanding concepts to assist them in taking their businesses to the next level. There are more than 40 seminars in all, and each year we try to maximize the number of experiential learning opportunities.

And we will be celebrating! This 10th Anniversary event is a one-time opportunity to come together with friends and colleagues to celebrate the end of the decade that launched the coaching profession and saw it reach new levels of professionalism. We would love for you to be part of this.

To register for the conference and get the discounted early-bird rate, go to http://www.coachfederation.org/conference/sanjose05/registration.asp before the end of the month.

I look forward to seeing you in San Jose.

Yours truly,

Steve Mitten, CPCC, MCC
ICF President
