Introductory Coaching Course

Atlanta, GA | February 19-21, 2010


Are you...

  • a professional considering coaching as a career?
  • a leader committed to bringing out the best in your employees and teams?
  • a learning professional whose position includes providing formal or informal coaching?

Co-active Fundamentals

If so, The Coaches Training Institutes® (CTI), the world’s undisputed leader in coach training, has the course for you. In this two-and-a-half-day course, nothing is held back. Course participants get the opportunity to explore Co-active Coaching in a small group, where the focus is on active participants and practical experience rather than lecture.

Over the weekend, participants will also learn:

  • Listening techniques to expand awareness and improve communication.
  • Powerful questions to empower and inspire action.
  • Intentional relationship design to get the most out of any working relationship.
  • How to expand leadership presence for greatest impact as a coach and beyond.

By the end of the program, participants will have concrete tools to immediately improve their effectiveness, as a well as a clear idea of how to incorporate coaching into their life and work.
