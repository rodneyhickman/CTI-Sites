About CTI's Coach Training Program
CTI's Co-Active Coach Training program is a four-course series. Each course focuses on developing specific skill sets that will help you uncover personal attributes that allow you to discover your unique style and voice as a coach. As you experienced in the Fundamentals course, most of the learning is “hands-on,” which provides students with plenty of coach/client practice time.

Benefits To You
When you complete the Co-Active Coach Training program, you will be able to:

  • Help clients define a vision of the future they will create.

  • Help your clients see many useful perspectives.

  • Use new techniques to get clients into live, sustainable, effective action.

  • Use structures to keep the client in action.

  • Incorporate your own creativity and ability into what you have learned in previous courses.

  • And more...
