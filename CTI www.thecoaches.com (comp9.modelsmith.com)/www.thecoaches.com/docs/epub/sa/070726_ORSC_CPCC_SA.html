The two-day Organization & Relationship Systems Coaching (ORSC) Fundamentals is an introduction to Relationship Systems Coaching and is an advanced level course designed to introduce you to a coaching specialization that will provide you with new marketable skills for your practice. Become a Relationship Systems Coach with competency in coaching business partners, teams, groups, boards, families and/or couples. The unique contribution of the ORSC model is that it provides coaches, consultants, therapists, facilitators and more, with a way to work directly with the system rather than do individual work within the system. If you want to increase your skills in listening for “the voice of the system” and working with that, this course is foundational! For more information about the Organization & Relationship Systems Coaching curriculum, visit www.centerforrightrelationship.com

In this Organization & Relationship Systems Coaching Fundamentals course, you will:
  • Learn how to create client awareness of the Third Entity—the relationship itself - then practice coaching the Third Entity directly
  • Gain competency in listening for, and working with the “Voice of the System.”
  • Coach conflict using the power of alignment coaching skills.
  • Gain awareness of and skills to use with the behaviors that compromise relationship system sustainability
  • Learn and practice skills and principles that have relationships flourish.
This is the first course in the Organization & Relationship Systems Coaching curriculum, and it is a pre-requisite for the remainder of the curriculum. In order to be eligible for this course, you need to have completed 82 coach training hours with CTI or another accredited coach training program; or be in an allied or related profession such as mediation, counseling, organizational development, mental health, etc. For allied professions mentioned before, experience in working with groups, teams or families is a requirement. The course provides 12 ICF CCEUs.
