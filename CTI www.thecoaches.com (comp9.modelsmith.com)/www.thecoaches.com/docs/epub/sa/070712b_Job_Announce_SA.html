CONTROLLER JOB SPECIFICATION

You will be responsible for reporting and analyzing the financial results for the Company in compliance with all federal, state, local and professional requirements.

General Responsibilities
Develop and maintain accounting systems for the company in maintaining books of accounts and related materials, as well as handling day-to-day financial matters.

Develop annual budget and quarterly forecasts, which requires strong Excel skills, knowledge of the business and interacting at all levels throughout the Company.

Monitor financial results versus budget and forecasts throughout the year and report any significant deviations to management with analysis detailing the cause for the deviation and corrective actions as necessary.

Responsible for timely monthly and year-end close, as well as coordinating work for all required audits or reviews.

Supervisory Responsibilities
Supervise Accounting Manager and two staff accountants. Plan, schedule and distribute incoming work to appropriate individuals. Develop and maintain an effective organization through the training, motivation, and review of direct staff. Provide necessary training for using accounting systems and procedures. Produce technical assistance and advice to accounting staff to ensure smooth and efficient day to day operations. Provide guidance and direction to accounting staff to assist them in their professional development. Assist with accounting department responsibilities as needed.

Qualifications
Experience using QuickBooks
Strong communication skills
Strong interpersonal skills

Budgeting, financial analysis, general ledger experience, previous supervisory experience is required. CPA is highly desired.

Compensation
Compensation is commensurate with skills and experience.

Location
This is a full-time position, based out of The Coaches Training Institute's main office located in San Rafael, CA.

INSIDE SALES MANAGER JOB SPECIFICATION

CTI is rapidly expanding its sales and marketing group and is seeking an Inside Sales Manager to join our team. The Inside Sales Manager will report to the VP of Sales & Marketing and holds responsibility for the day-to-day management of a telesales team against individual and group performance goals.

General Responsibilities

  • Achieve against aggressive team and organizational revenue goals
  • Partner with marketing and product development teams to develop resources for sales staff to use during the sales process (e.g. campaigns, emails, faxes, merchandisers, etc.)
  • Reliably forecast revenue on a monthly and quarterly basis
  • Serve as a resource for scripting and objection handling related to the sales process
  • Lead all sales staff content training and upskilling.
  • Stay current and knowledgeable on all CTI program offerings

Qualifications

  • 5-7 years experience managing an inside sales function
  • Experience managing outbound sales staff productivity and selling process
  • Experience utilizing database marketing and internet advertising technologies to drive revenue
  • Strong organizational skills and the ability to effectively manage multiple projects simultaneously
  • Excellent communication skills, verbal and written
  • Ability to create and deliver convincing arguments
  • Experience working in the leadership development or corporate training industry is a plus

Compensation
Compensation is commensurate with skills and experience.

This is a full-time position based out of The Coaches Training Institute's main office located in San Rafael, CA.
