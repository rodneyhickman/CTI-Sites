What is "Relationship Dojo"?

A dojo is the training hall in martial arts where sweaty, physical rigor meets the inner stillness of spiritual practice. Relationship Dojo is a new course in the Relationship Systems Coaching Program and, much like a martial arts dojo, it provides a learning space where you can deepen and hone the skills of Relationship Systems Coaching. This three-day advanced course is open only to those who have completed the Relationship Systems Coaching curriculum through The Way of Relationship.

Don't miss this opportunity to participate in the first offering of this advanced Relationship Systems Coaching course!

Relationship Dojo integrates the dimensions of Relationship River, Relationship Geography and The Way of Relationship through hands-on practice, group supervision, a deepened understanding of the underpinnings of the model and practice, practice, practice.

In the Relationship Dojo course, you will:

  • Deepen your understanding of the relationship dimensions of Relationship River, Relationship Geography and The Way of Relationship, and their different contributions to Relationship Systems Coaching.
  • Practice integrating the skills and tools of Relationship River, Relationship Geography and The Way of Relationship so that these can be utilized fluidly and flexibly, as the situation demands.
  • Practice, practice, practice!
  • Increase your confidence as a Relationship Systems Coach.
  • Add new practical tools and skills to your coaching toolkit.
  • Receive additional training in group coaching.
  • Practice, practice, practice!
  • Create community and have fun!

The cost of the Relationship Dojo course is $895.

Prerequisites:

  • You must have completed the Relationship Systems Coaching courses through The Way of Relationship.
  • Read and re-read the manual.
  • Have a relationship client to coach on Saturday.

This is the first time this course is being offered. In the future, it will be offered approximately once each year. To register, call The Coaches Training Institute at 1-800-691-6008.
