Co-Active Relationship Systems Coaching
Minneapolis, MN, metro area
Course dates: September 11–12, 2004

The two-day Relationship Systems Coaching Fundamentals is an introduction to relationship systems coaching and is an advanced-level course designed to introduce you to a coaching specialization that will provide you with new marketable skills for your practice. Become a Relationship Coach with skills for coaching business partners, teams, groups, boards, families and/or couples. These skills are applicable to all relationships and will enable you to specialize in a number of vibrant niche markets or expand your current role in organizational settings. For more information about CTI's Relationship Systems Coaching curriculum, visit http://www.thecoaches.com/relationship.

In this Relationship Systems Coaching Fundamentals course, you will:

  • Discover the power of alignment coaching skills.
  • Learn how to create client awareness of the third entity-the relationship itself.
  • Practice coaching the "third entity."
  • Develop new skills that you will use to adapt your intake process specifically for relationship coaching.
  • Gain awareness of and skills to use with the behaviors that blight relationships.
  • Learn and practice skills and principles that can help relationships flourish.
