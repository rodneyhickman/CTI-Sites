Designed to "build muscle around clarity," this powerful program lets participants tap into their inner leader, identify their true purpose and then move forward with confidence and grace by answering these three questions:

  • Who am I? "An exploration of your authentic self"
  • Where am I going? "The compelling path that pulls you forward"

Quest will help participants discover their authentic selves, understand where they are going and teach them the necessary skills they'll need to make their purpose real in the world.
