About Fulfillment
Fulfillment coaching is about helping clients let go of preconceived rules and limitations so that they can be open to what truly brings them alive. This course includes practical tools for helping clients discover their unique values, identify self-defeating influences and create their vision for a compelling future.

Taking this course, you will learn to:

  • Use visualization and imagery to help clients define a vision of the future they will create.
  • Assist clients to identify their unique values and assess their satisfaction with living those values.
  • Identify a client's self-defeating influences.
  • Design the relationship with your client.
  • Design an initial "intake" session.
