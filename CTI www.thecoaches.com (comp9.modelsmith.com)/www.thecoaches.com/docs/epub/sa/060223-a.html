FUNDAMENTALS

This course is foundational to the Co-Active Coaching curriculum. It is also a course that stands on its own if you want to learn coaching skills, but don't necessarily want to become a professional coach. There are no prerequisites to take this course.

The purpose of this course is to provide training and practical experience in the techniques and skills of the coaching profession, specifically Co-Active Coaching. This course is oriented toward participation and practical experience rather than lecture. You will have extensive opportunities to coach and to receive feedback on your coaching. For more information about this course go to: http://www.thecoaches.com/coaching/index.html

In this this course you will learn to:

  • Use the four cornerstones of Co-Active Coaching with your clients.
  • Communicate with your clients using the five contexts of Co-Active Coaching.
  • Use the Life (Balance) wheel with clients.
  • Distinguish and use the three levels of listening.
  • Demonstrate the most often used coaching skills.
