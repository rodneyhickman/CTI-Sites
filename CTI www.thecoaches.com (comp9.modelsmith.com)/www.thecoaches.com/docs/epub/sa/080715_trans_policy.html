To All Certification Students:

We at the Coaches Training Institute understand the need for flexibility when you schedule course dates with CTI. And while we will always support and recognize our students' needs, we also have to maintain our program policies to ensure the structure and design of our program settings.

CTI has established student-to-teacher ratios that have proven to provide a powerful and lasting experience for our students. When transfers occur less than 60 days prior to a Certification program start date, it truly impacts CTI and the other participants enrolled in that particular course. We want to maintain the integrity that CTI is known for and continue to provide all of our students with an experience that promotes the process of lifelong learning.

Consistent with this, we are revising our transfer policy, effective September 1, 2008.

You will be allowed up to two transfers at no charge, as long as the transfer is made 60 days prior to the start of your Certification course. Transfers made less than 60 days prior to the first day of the month your Certification program begins will incur a transfer fee of $175.

Should you have any questions regarding this change, please feel free to contact Barbara Ainslie, Certification Program Specialist, at 415-526-1620.
