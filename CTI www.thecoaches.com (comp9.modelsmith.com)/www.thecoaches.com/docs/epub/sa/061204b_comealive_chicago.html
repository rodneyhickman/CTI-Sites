Think of coaching at its best. You're in an intimate dance with your client, your timing is perfect, creativity sparks and insight flows effortlessly. This workshop is for coaches who want to tap into the joyful power of play in their lives and practice.

In Come Alive! The Artful Play of Coaching, you will learn to:

  • Liberate your own inherently playful nature!

  • Graduate from being a great coach to being a masterful coach!

Prerequisites:
The "Come Alive" in-person course is open to coaches who have an ACC, PCC or MCC accreditation from the ICF or who have completed Process in the CTI core coaching curriculum. The course carries 17.5 ICF Continuing Coaching Education Units.

About the course creators:

Henry Kimsey-House is co-founder of The Coaches Training Institute (CTI), co-author of the industry best-seller Co-Active Coaching and the main designer of CTI’s coaching curriculum. 

Gwen Gordon is an Emmy-award-winning writer who began a life of play designing the Muppets and now guides people in play as a path of awakening.
