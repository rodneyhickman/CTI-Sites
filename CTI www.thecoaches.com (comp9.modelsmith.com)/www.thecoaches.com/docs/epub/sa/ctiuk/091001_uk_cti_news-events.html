CTI UK News and Events

Dear Friend,

Amidst the hive of activity that is the Co-active community, there were a couple of important announcements that we regrettably forgot to include in our last email newsletter.

Firstly, the next European Co-active Community Day is on October 16 in London. As you probably know, coaches are often most alive and passionate when they are in each other's company. So if you want to give your confidence a boost and form new networks, be sure to book your place. See below for more information and a link to the European Co-active Community website.

We are also delighted to announce that we will be hosting a full coach training course (Fundamentals and Core Curriculum) in Manchester, North West England, in February 2010. We have known for a long time that some people who live north of Birmingham find the travel costs to our London courses prohibitive and so hope that through this initiative we will be able to significantly expand the UK Co-active community. This initiative has been aided significantly by the leadership of Kate Jones, of Inspired Lives (www.inspiredlives.com), who we featured in the email newsletter, in our 'Profile of a Successful Coach' section. She has been instrumental in helping hold the focus in the Co-active community in the North West of England for many years, and has ensured a seamless transition to the North West. Watch this space for further announcements.

And, finally, for those of you who missed the announcement in the last email newsletter, CTI is offering a new course for CPCCs. Entitled Leadership Circle, the course offers certification in the only 360-degree feedback instrument that measures the two primary leadership domains -- Creative Competencies and Reactive Tendencies -- and integrates this information so that the key opportunities for development immediately rise to the surface. The course, which will run 17-19 November, is also ICF-approved for CCEUs (continuing coaching education units). See below for more information.

European Co-active Coaching Community (ECCC)

Boost Your Confidence AND Grow Your Coaching Business!

10 am - 6:30 pm

Tower Hill, London

Boost your Confidence: Move from Fear to Fulfilment & Fun

Led by Nick Martin and Boris Prigmore

Boris and Nick will explore 'Confidence' and how you can have more of it! They will create an empowering space for you to discover an expanding sense of self and look at how you can apply the learning in the real world.

The Whole Self Approach to Building Your Coaching Practice: Confidence, Leadership, Business Building

Led by Beth Follini and Kate Griffiths

This session will focus on who you are 'being' as a coach and as a leader in order to build your coaching practice. Using their 'Whole Self Leadership Model,' Kate and Beth will engage you in experiential exercises that focus on the four key areas of mind, body, spirit and emotions.

The Heart of Social Networking: Connecting in the Virtual Village --Demystifying the World of Twitter

Led by Corrina Gordon-Barnes

Does the term 'social networking' get your technophobic gremlins over-excited and send you to overwhelm? This practical workshop simplifies and demystifies the world of Twitter, one of the most popular social networking forums. Want to sharpen your key marketing message? Hone your 'elevator pitch?' Reach your ideal clients with laser-beam focus? Enjoy this experiential welcome to the virtual village, where networking is based on a foundation of interdependence and win-win, and where fun, discovery and connection are the keys to success.

Growing Your Business Sustainably: Notes from the Bleeding Edge

Led by Deborah Huisken

What does it take to make your business sustainable -- for both yourself and for the long-term viability of your work? What does it take to refocus and rebuild your business after time out? In this workshop, Deborah Huisken shares learning and tips from her experience as an international coach newly returned to London. It captures the essence of a real-time 'work in progress' as she rebuilds her business a year after the death of her mother.

The Leadership Circle®

Now Your Client's Opportunities for Development Rise to the Surface Instantly

17-19 November, 2009, London, UK

The Leadership Circle Profile® is the only 360-degree feedback instrument that measures the two primary leadership domains -- Creative Competencies and Reactive Tendencies -- and integrates this information so that the key opportunities for development immediately rise to the surface.

Creative Competencies are well-researched competencies that measure how you achieve results, bring out the best in others, lead with vision, enhance your own development, act with integrity and courage, and improve organizational systems.

Reactive Tendencies are leadership styles that emphasize caution over creating results, self-protection over productive engagement and aggression over building alignment. These self-limiting styles overemphasize the focus on gaining the approval of others, protecting yourself and getting results through high control tactics.

Benefits

• Reveals to the leader what is going on beneath the surface and what may be keeping them stuck in outdated modes of behaviour.

• Evokes deeper personal awareness. Change can only happen when awareness changes.

• Enhances the value of coaching and saves time. The coach instantly sees an integrated picture, which reveals insights very quickly. Coaching can then start at an advanced level.

• Is an important tool for ongoing performance management.

• Aids in developing teamwork and alignment within management teams.

• Provides a comprehensive metric for tracking leadership development and cultural change.

• Aids in succession planning for developing high potential leaders.

• Allows you to measure your organization against a large norm-base of similar companies.

Advantages

More Revealing

It is the only leadership assessment tool that links an executive's leadership competencies to core beliefs that govern behavior.

More Advanced

It instantly reveals key opportunities for your client's development without requiring you to read through reams of documentation. TLCP enables you to start at the breakthrough conversation rather than spend months trying to reach one.

More Than Just an Assessment

The rich theoretical underpinnings of TLCP provide a pathway to transformation. No other tool of this type offers this.

More Efficient

TLCP is remarkably easy to use. It allows you to meet your client 'where they are,' without spending valuable coaching time finding out where that is. More Dynamic Seasoned consultants and executive coaches consistently report that TLCP enables them to have the most rewarding feedback sessions of their careers.

Widely Accepted

TLCP is world-class. It is used by leading national and international organizations, government agencies, and top universities.
