Customer Service Representative

Overview
The Customer Service Representative is responsible for all activities related to program registrations, which occur by phone, fax and email. In this role, you will register customers for our programs, process payments, maintain complete and accurate customer records, facilitate program transfers, and interact with customers regarding program-related matters. Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to create a connection with customers are vital in this position.

Skills

  • Strong execution and interpersonal skills
  • Highly organized
  • Strong attention to details
  • Ability to manage multiple responsibilities and set priorities
  • Experience responding to customers
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Proactive self-starter
  • Flexibility to adapt to a rapidly changing environment
  • Computer proficiency, including Word, Excel, Outlook, Filemaker

Experience and Education

  • The position requires an organized team player with 2-3 years' experience in a professional services environment.
  • College degree desirable.

Additional Information
This position is full-time, 8 am to 4:30 pm. Benefits include medical, dental, vision, 401k, FSA, life and disability insurance.
 
Compensation
Compensation is commensurate with skills and experience.
