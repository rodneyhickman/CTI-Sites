CTI has open slots for course assistants! As a participant of the Certification Program, you get priority when you request an assisting position for a course.

Assisting in Certification is a win/win for you and for CTI. The assisting experience will deepen your learning of the principles and skills of the Certification Program even more, and as a Certification participant, you will be a valuable and dependable assistant for CTI.  

While assisting is not a requirement of Certification, it is highly recommended. Past assistants have told us that they experienced the course information in a whole different way while assisting at the back of the room. It is also a great way to get to know our CTI leaders and a fabulous opportunity to witness our famous Co-Active leadership style from behind the scenes.  

You may choose to assist at any course you have completed as a way to deepen your learning. Occasionally, a supervisor may recommend that you assist at a course in order to improve your coaching skills in a particular area.

Again, we do have openings for assisting at this time. Since assisting is such a popular experience, please keep in mind that assistant slots usually fill three or four months in advance, so sign up early.

How do I sign up to assist?
To submit a request to assist, go to the CTI website and complete the application to assist. Once your request is received at CTI, someone from the logistics staff will get back to you.

Please do not call CTI and ask when we need assistants. CTI will let you know if assistants are needed for different dates or courses in your area.
