This summer, CTI launched a new program: Quest--A Leader's Journey. After two very successful West Coast programs, we've decided to take Quest on the road. We're headed for the East Coast and we'd LOVE to see you there.

About Quest
We are SO excited about this new program. Quest is like nothing CTI has ever done before, and the positive response from participants (130 so far!) has been thrilling!

Over the course of three days, Quest participants will embark on a journey into their own personal leadership. Designed to "build muscle around clarity," the program helps participants access the leader within themselves, identify their true purpose and move forward with confidence and grace by answering the questions:

  • Who am I? "An exploration of your authentic self"
  • Where am I going? "The compelling path that pulls you forward"
  • How do I get there? "Bringing your sense of purpose to the world"

Participants will become deeply intimate with the essence of themselves, the center of what is most important to them, and discover ways to begin bringing their authenticity and purpose more fully into the world. Quest contains all new content and a brand new format. In this program, we will be working with a large group while still holding the intimacy and power of a CTI experience.
