Systems Coaching Integration: Moving Toward Mastery (previously called Relationship Dojo) is a new course in CTI's Organization and Relationship Systems Coaching program. It provides a learning space in which you can deepen and hone the skills of Organization and Relationship Systems Coaching. This three-day advanced course is open only to those who have completed the Organization and Relationship Systems Coaching curriculum through Relationship Path: Vision and Potentiality. (Relationship Path was previously called The Way of Relationship. If you have completed The Way of Relationship, you are eligible to take Systems Coaching Integration.)

Systems Coaching Integration: Moving Toward Mastery integrates the dimensions of Relationship Intelligence, System Geography and Relationship Path through hands-on practice, group supervision, a deepened understanding of the underpinning of the model and practice, practice, practice.

In the Systems Coaching Integration course, you will:

  • Deepen your understanding of the relationship dimensions of Relationship Intelligence, System Geography and Relationship Path and their different contributions to Organization and Relationship Systems Coaching.
  • Practice integrating the skills and tools of Relationship Intelligence, System Geography and Relationship Path so that you can use them fluidly and flexibly, as the situation demands.
  • Practice, practice, practice!
  • Increase your confidence as Organization and Relationship Systems coaches.
  • Add new practical tools and skills to your coaching toolkit.
  • Receive additional training in group coaching.
  • Practice, practice, practice!
  • Create community and have fun!
