Co-Active Organization and Relationship Systems Coaching
San Francisco area
Course dates: November 5-6, 2005

The two-day Organization and Relationship Systems Coaching Fundamentals is an introduction to Relationship Systems Coaching and is an advanced-level course designed to introduce you to a coaching specialization that will provide you with new marketable skills for your practice. Become a relationship coach with skills for coaching business partners, teams, groups, boards, families and/or couples. These skills are applicable to all relationships and will enable you to specialize in a number of vibrant niche markets or expand your current role in organizational settings. For more information about CTI's Organization and Relationship Systems Coaching curriculum, visit www.thecoaches.com/relationship.

In this Organization and Relationship Systems Coaching Fundamentals course, you will:

  • Discover the power of alignment coaching skills.
  • Learn how to create client awareness of the "third entity"—the relationship itself.
  • Practice coaching the "third entity."
  • Develop new skills that you will use to adapt your intake process specifically for relationship coaching.
  • Gain awareness of and skills to use with the behaviors that blight relationships.
  • Learn and practice skills and principles that can help relationships flourish.
