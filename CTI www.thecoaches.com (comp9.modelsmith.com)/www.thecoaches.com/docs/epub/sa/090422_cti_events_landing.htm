Returning to Wholeness
May 20th, 7:00pm, ICF Conference
Los Angeles, CA


The state of the global economy has shaken us all loose from ‘business as usual’ and provided a spectacular moment of global consciousness. We are beginning to see clearly that as residents of this planet, we are profoundly and inextricably interdependent. Much of the foundation of what we know is shifting leaving us all unusually free to improvise a fresh course forward.

Leaders from every realm of our world — business, secular and spiritual have acknowledged this time as one of great transition and potential transformation. It is a time of powerful choice where we will either be dominated by the circumstances or be shaped and changed by the challenges that face us.

Please join CTI co-founder Karen Kimsey-House for an evening of conversation, inquiry and exploration. What will you create from this time? What is the role that you, as a coach, choose to play in the unfolding new story of our world?

Embracing Your Shadow
1-Day Workshop

Saturday May 2, 2009
9:00 AM - 5:00 PM PT
San Francisco, CA


In this one-day workshop, New York Times bestselling author Debbie Ford will guide us into the heart of the duality that unknowingly operates within each one of us: the force that compels us to live by our values, and give and receive love; and the force that holds us back, sabotages our efforts and repeatedly steers us toward bad choices. Debbie will explain the origins of self-destructive behavior and the battle between our light and dark sides.

She will help us understand how we end up damaging the lives we've worked so hard to create, explaining the masks we wear to protect ourselves, including the People Pleaser, the Victim, the Bully, Mister Cool and the Jokester. Understanding these masks and what they cover up allows us to go beneath the surface, wake up from denial, and become the person we always intended to be. Providing the tools to unlock the patterns of self-sabotage, Debbie shows us how to heal the split between light and dark, and live the authentic life that is within our reach.

Join Debbie - a true master at finding the gold inside the dark and the wisdom inside your pain and disappointments - for a day that promises to open up unforeseen possibilities and expose the only thing that stands between you and the life you deeply desire: your shadow. Discover the profound secret of your shadow and unlock the confidence, power, creativity and freedom to fully be your most magnificent self. Mine the gifts hidden in your past… and find yourself standing at the threshold of a brand new life.
