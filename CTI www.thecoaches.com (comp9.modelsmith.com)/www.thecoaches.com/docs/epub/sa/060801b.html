About the CTI curriculum:

CTI’s core coaching curriculum consists of five in-person, experiential weekend programmes that provide strong grounding and practice in the principles of Co-Active coaching, within a limited class size of 26. Our programmes are co-led by two CTI faculty leaders, who meet the profession’s most rigorous training, selection and quality performance standards. They consistently receive rave reviews in student curriculum evaluations. Completion of the curriculum prepares the participant for application to CTI’s coaching certification programme, accredited by the International Coach Federation.
