Don't miss R5 this June -- led by Karen and Henry Kimsey-House and held in the south of France!

Take advantage of this week-long course in one of the most breathtaking venues CTI has ever offered. Experience the magic of being led by Karen and Henry Kimsey-House, co-founders of CTI. Karen and Henry rarely lead together, and this will no doubt be a unique experience on many levels.

Leadership Mastery (Retreat 5) will be held at the Domaine d'Escaunes on June 15! Situated in the center of the Avignon-Nimes-Uzes triangle, the Domaine d'Escaunes is an ancient royal hostelry (staging post), dating from the 16th century and surrounded by vineyards and olive trees.

Retreat 5 expands upon and extends the leadership skills you developed in the four retreats of the Co-Active Leadership Training program. Some of the skills you will hone during this retreat include the following:

  • Deepening full permission

  • Taking responsibility for impact

  • Creating from everything (an unforgettable experience!)

You will learn new techniques for more accurately reading and interacting with Level 3 leaders, experience exponential growth in co-leading, and have ample opportunity to practice Level 4 leadership and feedback.
