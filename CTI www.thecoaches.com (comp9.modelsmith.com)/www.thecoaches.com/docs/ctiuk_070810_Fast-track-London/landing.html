Cut short your coach training by 13 weeks this Fall in London, England.
September 17th – October 28th

Last year we launched our new Fast Track Co-active® Coach Training program for people who were called to coaching but had been put off by the time scales required. Not everyone wants to wait 5-6 months to begin the accreditation process as a coach. Neither do managers want to wait to be able to apply powerful communication and relationship building skills in the workplace.

So, at 6-7 weeks in duration our Fast Track program provides the perfect solution – and although the course is described as Fast, we don’t mean hasty:

  • There are no techniques employed during Fast Track designed to artificially speed up retention of knowledge
  • The difference is simply the overall time it takes to complete the course
  • The number of ICF accredited hours (104) covered remains the same, as does the content and learning style of the program
  • Participants experience 2 modules at a time, back to back, with a break of a couple of weeks in between, with the fifth module also running after an interim break

So, we have decided to run the Fast Track program in both the UK and the US at different times of the year.

Our next program will be in the Fall between September 17th – October 28th in London, England.
