Retreat Locations

Northern California, USA

All retreats are held at a beautiful, modern Northern California retreat center in Sonoma County, approximately two hours north of the San Francisco International Airport (SFO) and five miles inland from the Pacific Ocean. A free shuttle service is provided from several hotels near SFO the morning of the first retreat, returning to SFO on Sunday at approximately 5:00 PM. Participants are otherwise responsible for their own transportation to and from the retreat center.

North Carolina, USA

All retreats are held at a beautifully renovated 50 year old tobacco barn nestled on a 63 acre Green farm in the Blue Ridge Mountains of Western North Carolina. The Lodge is located about 35 minutes from the Asheville, NC airport. A free shuttle service is provided to and from the airport for your first retreat. Participants are otherwise responsible for their own transportation to and from the Lodge.

Sitges, Spain

All retreats are held at a beautifully restored masia near Sitges, Spain, less than one hour from the Barcelona airport. Transportation is provided from several locations between the airport and masia the morning of the first retreat, returning to the airport on the final day of the first retreat at approximately 4:30 PM. Participants are otherwise responsible for their own transportation to and from the retreat center.

In choosing a program, please be sure that you can attend all four retreats of that program. You will have the same leaders and fellow participants throughout the course of the program.

In order to be put on the waiting list for a full program, you must be registered in another open program.
