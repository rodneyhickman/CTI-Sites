Registration

Program Fees

The total program cost is $11,900.00 (US programs) or €11,500.00 (European programs). This cost includes tuition, meals and residential fees for all four retreats. Payment options are available for the balance, provided 25% of the total is paid at least three weeks prior to each of the four retreats. Invoices are available upon request. CTI only accepts U.S. funds. Exchange rate is determined by your financial institution on the date of the transaction. CTI is not responsible for any fluctuations or transaction fees charged by your financial institution.

Registration

An $975.00 deposit (US programs) or €975.00 (European programs) is required to reserve a space in the program of your choice, payable by check (US only), credit card or wire transfer. This payment will be applied toward tuition and/or fees in the event of a cancellation or transfer.
