Scholarship FAQs

What are the prerequisites?

The applicant must be referred by a CTI faculty member or a CTI Program Advisor. The Co-Active Coaching Fundamentals course is not a requirement.

What are the amounts offered?

The Scholarship Committee will decide the amounts of the scholarships offered. They will not be full scholarships as we expect participants to also make an investment in the program.

What are the expectations from CTI?

Complete and full out participation in the program is required. The participant will be held accountable by their Leadership program leaders. We expect that the participant will have a concrete reason for participating in Leadership and by the end of the program will report back on how this training will be used in the world.

How are recipients chosen?

It will be a committee decision based on the individual applicant, the pool of applicants being reviewed and the money available in the scholarship fund.

What is the process?

The application is available here. Please follow the instructions. After reviewing the application and instructions, if you have question, please contact a Program Advisor for additional information. There will be an application review by CTI Scholarship Committee and there may be a phone interview, as well. The committee decides the amount of the scholarship.

Is this open to International participants?

This is open to the US run programs in the North America and Spain locations. Contact us with any questions about this.

How long until I know?

The CTI Scholarship Committee will be reviewing applications quarterly, based on availability of funds.
