Creating From Self Program

Have you ever considered participating in the Co-Active Leadership Program yet hesitated to enroll due to concerns about the time commitment or the financial cost? If so, CTI has come up with a way to experience what we mean by Co-Active leadership that addresses both of these issues: we will be offering the first retreat from the Co-Active Leadership Program series on a standalone basis in June 2012.

Experience the first powerful leadership piece called “Creating from Self,” without committing to the full four retreat program.

Gain a deeper understanding of yourself and your path in your world or community. Break through self-perceived limits and gain confidence that comes from knowing yourself. Do this work in a community of like-minded peers who will hold you to your true magnificence even when you can’t see it yourself. Discover new ways to be in relationship in all areas of your life.

 

This retreat will be offered in two locations - California, US and Sitges, Spain.

Dates:June 20-25, 2012June 17-22, 2012
Location:Sonoma County, CASitges, Spain
Venue:Westerbeke RanchAlmiral de la Font
Cost:$2995 USD€2995 Euros

 

If you have ever been interested in finding out what the Co-Active Leadership program is all about, this is your chance. It’s important to sign up soon, there are only 20 spots left for this program and we expect them to fill quickly. 

No discount coupons may be applied to this special program, however, if you decide to continue on with the full program, discount coupons may be used. The amount paid for the standalone retreat will be credited toward full tuition.

Contact a Program Advisor right away at 415-451-6000 option 3 or 800-691-6008 option 3 to register or learn more. You can also email info@thecoaches.com.
