Leadership Mastery

Leadership Mastery (Retreat 5) expands upon and extends leadership skills developed in the four retreats of the Co-Active Leadership Training program. Some of the skills honed during this retreat include Deepening of Full Permission, Taking Responsibility for Impact, and an unforgettable experience of Creating from Everything. You will learn new techniques for more accurately reading and interacting with Level 3 leaders, experience exponential growth in co-leading, and have ample opportunity to practice Level 4 leadership and feedback.

Outcomes:

  • Learn the vital importance of giving yourself full permission to fail
  • Build leadership 'muscle'
  • Learn how to Recover to Your Stake and Stay, even after you succeed
  • Practice coaching arcs and timing
  • Integrate your Level 3 awareness with your Level 1 urge

Pre-requisite

Completion of the Co-Active Leadership Training program (all four retreats).

Dates & Locations

Leadership Mastery begins at 11:00 AM on Day 1 and ends at 3:00 PM on Day 6.
