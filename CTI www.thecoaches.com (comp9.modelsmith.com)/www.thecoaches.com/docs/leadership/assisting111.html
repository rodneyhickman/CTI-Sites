CO-ACTIVE LEADERSHIP ASSISTANT GROUNDWORK

Congratulations on being chosen to assist in the Co-active leadership program. The materials on this page will help prepare you for that engagement. Please read though this page and bookmark the location so you can return here during your course for training and resources.

You have an exciting growth opportunity ahead of you and we thank you for your contribution to our community.

GETTING ORGANIZED

Create a folder for your leadership assisting materials. You might want an electronic folder but you will also need print materials with you at the retreat center so it is a good idea to print out the necessary documents as well, and bring them with you.

COMPLETE THE ONLINE FORMS

In order for us to confirm your placement, we must have the following forms completed by 1 month in advance of your Retreat 1 start date.

  1. Read the Agreements and sign the online form.
  2. Fill out your Assisting Questionnaire Online (download pdf to preview the questions before you go to the online form)
    Assistant-Questions.pdf
  3. Fill out the dietary needs form online to let us know your dietary requirements
  4. Complete the online medical form and sign the release of liability
