Bringing Out the Best in Others - Learning

Purpose

The Co-Active coaching model is a powerful framework for leaders to interact with others in a way that truly brings out the best. One of the contexts of Co-Active coaching is “Deepen the Learning and Forward the Action” representing a focus on the importance of both learning and action. The tools in this section support the learning process in particular.
