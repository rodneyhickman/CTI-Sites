Performance Enablement Champion Training

Welcome to your upcoming program with the Coaches Training Institute.  You are embarking on an important learning process that will impact your capacity to bring forward the best talent in all of your people.  The program includes an integrated set of components to foster your learning, provide you with a direct experience of coaching, and support you in bringing new skills directly to your role in the company. Your full engagement in this learning process can dramatically alter your capacity to bring out the best in those you work with.

The components include:

  • Up front self-assessment
  • 2.5 day experiential learning program
  • follow up learning labs conducted as teleclasses
  • one-on-one post program coaching
  • access to the leaders toolkit online
