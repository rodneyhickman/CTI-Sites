Careers at CTI

At CTI, we're working toward a future where everyone's potential can be fulfilled and we're growing fast through our impact.

Our strategy is simple: we want to hire great people and encourage them to make their dreams and aspirations a reality. CTI's culture is one of inclusion, respect, creativity and empowerment for everyone in the organization.

We are committed to being a great place to work. We are committed to making a big difference in the world. If you want substantial difference through your work, AND want to have fun in a challenging yet supportive environment, take a look at the positions we have currently available:

 

 

The Coaches Training Institute is an Equal Opportunity Employer

We are always searching for innovative, creative thinkers who are enthusiastic about their work and are committed to delivering excellence in serving our customers.

CTI is announcing recruitment for the position of Customer Service Supervisor with a minimum of three years’ experience who will lead the customer service team.  This position reports to CTI’s Director of Operations and requires a well developed pattern of outstanding customer service, competitive skills, energy, motivation, enthusiasm, and the integrity critical to success in this role. 

Under the direction of the Director of Operations, the holder of this position supervises and coordinates the day-to-day activities of the customer service team to ensure a superior level of service.  Maintains and improves customer service satisfaction by implementing operational practices and procedures and coordinating the daily workflow.   Ensures a high level of quality and responsiveness to customer questions and issues, maintains positive interdepartmental relationships and ensures that all departmental goals are achieved on a daily and monthly basis. Functions in a working supervisor capacity and performs a variety of general duties and responsibilities.

The holder of this position is required to build deep lifelong relationships with customers and provide outstanding customer service, being a team innovative supervisor.

The successful candidate must have extensive experience with all aspects of customer service.  Also, the candidate must have very strong written and verbal communication skills and must be able to create a thriving personally and professionally team work environment.

 

Principal Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Coordinates, supervises and is accountable for customer service team daily activities
  • Creates and leads a positive and enthusiastic work environment that is team-oriented, customer focused and quality driven
  • Supports and drives process improvement initiatives that contribute to overall customer satisfaction, operational excellence and company goals
  • Trains assigned staff, monitors efficiency and quality efforts
  • Supports a thriving learning culture by encouraging personal development
  • Conducts annual performance reviews for team members
  • Develop and implement customer service policies and procedures
  • Plans, prioritizes and delegates work tasks to ensure the efficient functioning of the team and performance goals are met
  • Answers incoming phone calls about course dates, location, price and curriculum content
  • Maintains complete and accurate customer records, processes payments, transfers and cancellations and emails relevant course communications
  • Interacts with customers regarding course logistics and refunds and resolves delinquent accounts
  • Resolves customer complaints and answers questions regarding policies
  • Provides administrative and technical support to the Co-Active Network

 

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:

  • Considerable knowledge of the day-to-day operations of customer service activities and service level expectations
  • Proven track record of building and developing a team of customer service professionals
  • Ability to instruct, direct, and evaluate employees
  • Excellent attention to detail, including an ability to analyze, interpret and evaluate complex issues and make appropriate recommendations
  • Customer service oriented, with ability to handle difficult situations with a  positive attitude
  • Ability to work in a fast paced environment, while managing multiple tasks.
  • Adaptable, with ability to accept and support change
  • Positive winning approach to the job
  • Effective communication skills that include active listening, rapport building and positive customer care.
  • Ability to follow directions and perform tasks correctly on a consistent basis.
  • Highly organized, with a strong attention to detail.
  • Ability to manage multiple responsibilities and set priorities.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Proactive self-starter.
  • Flexibility to adapt to a rapidly changing environment.
  • Computer proficiency, including Word, Excel, Outlook, Filemaker

 

Education and Required Experience

  • College  degree
  • More than three years of supervising experience

 

Please submit your resume by October 25, 2012 to humanresources@coactive.com
