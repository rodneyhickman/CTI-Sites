CTI is dedicated to your success as a coach in every way.  We’re very excited to announce the launch of a brand new CTI program, Breakaway Marketing for Coaching and to invite you to our event in the DC area on November 13-14.

CTI students have told us they want a proven, up-to-date, no-nonsense approach offered by marketing professionals and presented in a way that is experiential and fun.  So, in partnership with a top-notch marketing company, we’ve created Breakaway Marketing for Coaching to bring you the most effective techniques for business success in today’s changing market.

 

Breakaway Marketing for Coaching is designed to capitalize on the great coaching training you received from CTI. It's co-led and interactive with lots of hands-on learning, as well being packed full with up-to-date information and tools that you can begin using right away. In this two-day course, you will:

·       Create a custom-designed marketing plan for your coaching business.

·       Develop the skills and tools needed to implement that plan successfully.

·       Learn how to get new clients right away.

·       Discover specific strategies to engage visitors on your website.

·       Learn how social networking on LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter can bring in new business.
