Hello Beloved Co-Active Leaders:

It’s time to open up the RFP (Request for Proposal) process for the 2014 Summit! We’re hugely excited about how things are shaping up and are eager to hear your creative ideas and thinking.

There are a few things that are important for you to know about this RFP process:

  1. We are keeping Breakouts to a minimum for the next Summit so we are only having a few spots.
  2. We are taking great care with the arc and overall story of the Summit so are looking through a very specific lens.

The stake for the Summit is to create a shift from “me” to “we” and for us all to learn about how to open leadership so that it is held and shared collectively. We have all experienced moments of this… times when everyone was at 100% and the leadership moved fluidly around the circle.

We are looking for breakout presentations that provide both experience and technology to empower people to accomplish this fluidity, with tangible tools participants can begin using right away.

To summarize, we are looking for presentation ideas and topics that:

  1. Focus on the “we” the collective, rather than the individual.
  2. Introduce concepts, ideas and tools that are cutting edge and new to our population.
  3. Give people tangible tools and models that they can use to generate fluid group or team leadership as described above.
  4. Can be delivered for 100-150 people.
  5. Are appropriate for a 2.5 to 3 hour span of time.

If you have a topic or model you would like us to consider you must:

  1. Click here to download and complete the RFP form.
  2. Submit to coactivesummit@coactive.com by June 28, 2013 along with a Bio (limit 150 words) and Photo (attach a jpeg no more than 100KB).

We will respond before July 26.

Thanks for sharing your passion, creativity and heart with us! See you at the Summit.

Love,
