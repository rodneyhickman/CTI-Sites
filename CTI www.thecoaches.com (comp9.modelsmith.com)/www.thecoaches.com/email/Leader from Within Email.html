This October Leader from Within will be held in beautiful Sonoma, California. You’ll meet great people, eat fabulous food, enjoy a spectacular wine-county setting, and learn lots.

CTI is offering this opportunity—the first week of the full Leadership program—so that you can “try it on”. You should come if you can stand in a place of curiosity, exploration and experimentation about your own internal leader and want to participate fully in your own learning process.

Leader from Within

When: October 20-25, 2013
Where: Sonoma, California
Who’s Leading: Pat Carrington-House and Rick Tamlyn
How Much: $2995
Who should come: YOU

For a program as special as Leader from Within, we’ve found two extraordinary Co-Active Leaders to take you on this Leadership journey.  Rick Tamlyn, MFA, CPCC, MCC and Pat Carrington-House, CPCC, PCC will be your program leaders.  Rick and Pat are each very special as individuals and we have found that together, they are magic!

Pat Carrington-House, CPCC, PCC

Pat is a coach, leadership trainer, and an aspiring Kalahari Bushman. He leads seminars and workshops throughout the world, training people to be effective coaches, leaders, and agents of transformation. In 2002 he founded Dolphin Leadership Inc., a coaching and leadership training company that works with teams, individuals, and organizations. For the better part of his life, Pat has maintained a strong connection to the natural world and earth-based cosmological systems. One of the systems he has spent much of the last decade studying is a pre-Mayan system called the Delicate Lodge Teachings. He weaves this wisdom into all of his endeavors. The idea of play is hugely important to his life, and working with Pat all but guarantees there will be as much deep laughter as there will be deep learning.
   
 

Rick Tamlyn, MFA, CPCC, MCC

Rick is a sought-after leader in the human development field and works internationally as an experiential keynote speaker, workshop/seminar leader, and as a personal leadership coach. As a Co-Founder of ‘It’s All Made Up, Inc’. and ‘The Bigger Game,’ Rick has delivered over 300 Co-Active Coaching/Leadership workshops, Bigger Game workshops, and conference keynote sessions in both the public and private sectors. Along with CTI co-founder Laura Whitworth and colleague Caroline MacNeill Hall, Rick is co-author of the book The Bigger Game: Why Playing a Bigger Game Designs Who You Want to Become. Rick’s personal ‘Bigger Game’ is to develop and enhance leadership skills within individuals, organizations and communities, for the sake of a more fulfilled and sustainable world something for which our entire planet hungers.
