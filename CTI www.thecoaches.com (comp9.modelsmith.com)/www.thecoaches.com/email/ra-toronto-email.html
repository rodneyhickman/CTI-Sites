RELATIONSHIP AGILITY is about consciously creating relationships with others through better conversations, deeper understanding of one's own paradigms, and shared accountability. The program enables you to catch yourself and others operating in an unproductive mode and provides tools and skills to support getting back on track.


Who is RELATIONSHIP AGILITY for?

  • Individuals who want to deepen personal or professional relationship with others.
  • Organizations that want to enhance collaboration, communication and team work.
  • Managers who want to create shared accountability with colleagues & team members
  • Coaches who want continued professional development. {Note that this is not a coaching course and yet, if you are a coach, it provides skills and tools that you can apply to coaching your clients around relationship issues.}
