About Nancy

Nancy Conger, CPCC, PCC has been a coach since 1997. Her specialty is enlivening leaders by bringing out their natural creative powers. She has been on faculty with The Coaches Training Institute since 2001, where she leads courses throughout North America and supervises Certification students.
Nancy is an author, playwright, musician and visual artist. You may see her on stage performing her one-woman show “The Truth about Women and Horses,” or performing strolling violin at various events. She is currently crafting a musical.
