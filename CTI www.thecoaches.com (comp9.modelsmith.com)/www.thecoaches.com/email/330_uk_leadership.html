Join us for London's Inaugural Co-Active Leadership Alumni Peer Support Group...

From Sue (Walrus) and Phil (Kangaroo)


Greetings Leadership alumni!

We are excited. There comes a time when the tipping point in society is ripe for something new. Something’s gotta’ give, a movement is yearning to happen and ‘become’. We believe we are on the precipice of one of those movements right now, and we want to share it with you and include you.
RSVP Now
Date: 19th April, 2013
Location: London, UK
Time: 9:30 - 12:30 am

The leadership alumni in London is rich and diverse here and we feel ready to pilot something pretty special. On the 19th April we will be co-leading the very first local, face to face, CTI supported Co-Active Leadership Alumni initiative, provisionally titled 'Quest Connect.'

(For older alumni please know that Quest is the new name for leadership project.)

The Quest Connect meeting is unique and pioneering in that it’s being created and facilitated just for graduates of Leadership, all tribes, all years - and will be of interest to anyone who has completed CTI's 10 month leadership program.

And you’re invited. We want your presence.

Date and Time: 19th April, 9.30 a.m - 12.30 pm (with option for pub Lunch after)
Venue:  etc Venues, 51-53 Hatton Garden, London, (nearest tube, Farringdon).
RSVP: Sue Belton, sue@suebelton.com 

RSVP Now


Our intention for this initiative, if this pilot is successful, is to bring regularity to it and facilitate it quarterly (TBC). That way, a face to face event will exist that Leadership grads can plan for, contribute to and feel supported by. CTI will be supporting the continuity of this initiative by promoting it regularly to old and new graduates.

As the name suggests, ‘quest’ and the process of ‘questing’ however that looks for you is a core focus. You may have a very strong connection to your tribe mates or you may have lost touch, either way our urge is to create the space that encourages connection, rekindles the questing fire and sets it blazing!

We hope you can join us.

Phil Askew CPCC & Sue Belton CPCC
