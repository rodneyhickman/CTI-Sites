CTI DC invites you to co-create:

 

An Island of Sanity

In the everday ocean of confusion and compression 

 

You in particular are invited to come and co-create a new era in Co-Active coaching in Washington, DC. This will be a truly bigger game. Come and connect, receive, contribute, grow, create, lead. Come also to give back.

Island of Sanity H2O Framework:

Hour 1: we intend to create an Island of Sanity first for all the CTI coaches in the DC area, a protected monthly time and place where each and all can come to be refreshed and renewed – connect with each other, sharpen the tools you know, learn new tools, practice mindfully, coach and be coached, socialize in fun, be refreshed as naturally creative, resourceful, and whole. Then, renewed stay for Hour 2.

Hour 2 – we give back to the community: co-create with us an Island of Sanity for residents of DC, especially those who cannot typically afford coaching, and who are ready to grow. This will be a monthly time and place where they can come to receive excellent coaching. We want word to spread person to person: “Friend, go visit the Island of Sanity. You’ll be listened to like you’ve never been listened to. Life can get better.”

Inaugural meeting details:

Why: At this inaugural meeting we will create the “bones” of Hour 1 & 2 and plan for future events. Please bring something to contribute to the Island as there will be an initiation ceremony.
When: November 1st from 6-8pm.
Cost: Free! 
Register here: http://www.eventbrite.com/event/8335747445. Maximum of 50.
Where: Friends Meeting House, 2111 Florida Ave NW, Washington DC 20008 (202-483-3310) 
Directions: from the Q Street Exit on the Redline at Dupont Circle, walk 2 blocks north on Connecticut Ave NW, then a sharp left (southwest) for 1 block on Florida Ave NW, then a sharp right to enter on Decatur Pl NW

Note: We are grateful to the Washington Friends house for renting us this space.  Please feel free to bring anything (i.e. food + drink) to take care of your needs.  CTI will not be providing refreshments.
