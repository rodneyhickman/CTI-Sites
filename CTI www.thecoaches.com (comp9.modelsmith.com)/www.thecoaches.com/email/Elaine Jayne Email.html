Dear Co-Active Community;

It is with a potent mix of sadness, love and pride that I write to let you know that as of 2014, Elaine Jaynes will no longer be leading the Co-Active Leadership Program.  This is a major transition as Elaine has been leading this program almost since it's inception in 1996 and over the years has contributed in countless ways to the resonance and depth of the program.

In addition she had a loving hand in the birthing and transformation of many, many Co-Active Leaders around the world.  It is absolutely true that we simply would not be here without her love, wisdom and powerful leadership. Here are some words from Elaine:

"Building and re-building this program has been my primary work…the most creative and most compelling part of my professional identity…for years.  I have loved the discoveries, the expansions, the thought leadership.  I'm proud of that work and prouder still of the people I've co-created with along the way.  Every program, every Tribe has co-created its own diverse platform on which all the other tribes rest as they move forward.  I'm excited that I have been (and will certainly continue to be) part of the legacy of this program and of the Co-Active presence in our world.  I want to complete this round strongly and well.  And then it's time to move on.  I'll continue my coaching practice…exploring the alignment between Leadership and Spirit.  I'll likely show up here and there at CTI.  I am deeply exploring the concept (and the experience!) of Elderhood.  I have avoided writing for a lifetime.  Perhaps it is time now to apprentice myself to that particular something new."

Elaine will still be engaged with CTI and very much a part of our world and I felt it was important to mark this transition with all of you.  I know you will join me in celebrating Elaine and the impact she has had on our lives and our work.  

Also, I want to be sure that you all know that November 2013 will be the start of the last leadership program Elaine will lead.  If you want for someone you love to experience Elaine's particular brand of magic, this will be their last chance.

The beginning of the program will be November 10th-15th, 2013 in Barcelona, Spain.

To register, call Debra Martin at 415-526-1633 or email debra@coactive.com

Much love to all,

Karen
