CO-ACTIVE SUMMIT WHY CTI CTI HOME

Hello beloved Co-Active Leaders:

2014 Global Co-Active Summit
April 10-12, 2014
Meritage Resort & Spa
Napa, California

A few weeks ago, we (Henry and Karen) got together with Rick Tamlyn to finish up the design of the 2014 Global Co-Active Summit and all of us are so excited!

We see Co-Active Leadership as being central to the vision of Co-Active in the world, so Leadership will be the core theme of the upcoming Summit - with all of the keynotes and breakout sessions designed to grow leadership in all.

As a graduate of the Leadership Program, no one is better qualified than you and your tribe mates to participate with us and help grow the conversation about Co-Active Leadership in the world.

Of course we want EVERYONE to be at this upcoming Summit. We know it is going to be an important gathering of everyone in the CTI community, including leadership alumni, CPCCs, and all those who have taken courses through our open enrollment programs or through corporate training. However, we want as many of YOU to attend as possible and we’ve created some special things just for you. Keep reading!

We’re really jazzed about the idea of tribes having their Retreat Fives through Eighteens around the Co-Active Summit. We want to support you in creating whatever particular learning your tribe is wanting and also in connecting, networking and sharing resources with other tribes that will be meeting in rooms, restaurants and wineries right next to you! So here’s what we’ve planned:

    Co-Active Summit details

  • April 10-12, 2014
  • Held at the beautiful Meritage Resort & Spa in Napa, CA. Check it out!
  • We’ll have the WHOLE resort to ourselves during the Summit (can you imagine)?!
  • CTI will have discounted pricing on hotel rooms during the Summit and for three days before and after Summit, depending on availability.
  • Take advantage of an Early Bird registration price of $995 (reg. $1295) which is valid until August 31. This includes access to keynote speakers, breakout sessions, two lunches and a CTI party.
  • To register, call (415) 451-6000 (option 1) or 1-800-691-6008 (option 1) or visit www.coactivesummit.com

And just for you and your tribe mates we’ve arranged:

  • 8 event rooms for tribe meetings on April 7 and April 8, (9am-5pm) AT NO CHARGE. Because we have a limited number of event rooms available, they will be distributed on a first come, first served basis to the first 8 tribes who have a minimum of 8 members REGISTERED for the Co-Active Summit. Call (415) 451-6000 (option 1) or 1-800-691-6008 (option 1) to reserve an event room.
  • Once those event rooms are taken, there are many other places at the Meritage for tribe meetings – including in the courtyards, by the pool, or in the restaurants.
  • Henry and I will be designing a SPECIAL PRE-SUMMIT EVENT just for graduates of the Leadership program on Wednesday afternoon (April 9). This is just for graduates - AT NO CHARGE. This is our gift to you.
  • Finally, we are SO interested in your Leadership Quests and Projects, no matter how small or large, shallow or deep. To that end, we’re in the process of creating a Quest database at CTI and we will be presenting a Quest Prize at the Summit. Stay tuned for more information.

Imagine the energy at the Meritage as individual tribes gather and do their work! Imagine the energy when the WHOLE tribe of Leadership graduates gathers in one place for an entire afternoon on April 9. Now imagine all that energy launching itself into the Co-Active Summit the following day. . . WOW!

So heat up the phone lines. Get the tom tom drums beating. Call your tribe together to make plans for the 2014 Global Co-Active Summit and for the cool stuff that will be happening prior. You do need to get going...this is one case where the Co-Active early bird really will get the worm!

We love you, and we can’t wait to see you in person soon!

Karen and Henry
